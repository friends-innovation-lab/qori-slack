## Study Title
VA Health and Benefits Mobile App Navigation Study

## What problem are you trying to solve?
The VA mobile app has a 4.2/10 user satisfaction rating with 45% task abandonment rate. Veterans frequently use search to find basic features like prescription refills and appointment scheduling, indicating poor information architecture. We need to understand why veterans struggle to navigate the app and what changes would improve their experience.

## What decisions will this research inform?
This research will inform the Q1 2026 mobile app redesign, specifically: navigation structure (bottom tabs vs hamburger menu), information hierarchy, button placement, and accessibility improvements for screen reader users.

## Who are the target users?
Veterans who actively use the VA mobile app at least 2-3 times monthly for health and benefits management. Include users with accessibility needs (VoiceOver, TalkBack, large text). Mix of iOS and Android users across different age ranges and technical comfort levels.

## When do you need this?
End of August 2025

## Any additional context?
Recent analytics show 60% of users who abandon tasks end up calling the VA help line instead. Congressional oversight is increasing pressure to improve digital services. Accessibility compliance is mandatory.
