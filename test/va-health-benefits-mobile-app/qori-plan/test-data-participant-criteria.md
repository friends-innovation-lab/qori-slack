# Participant Criteria — VA Mobile App Navigation Study

## Must Have

- Active VA Mobile App user (used the app at least 3 times in the past 60 days)
- Veteran or active-duty service member with VA benefits
- Has completed at least one task in the app (claims, prescriptions, appointments, or messaging)
- Comfortable sharing their screen during a remote session
- At least 2 of 8 participants use assistive technology (VoiceOver, TalkBack, or switch control)
- At least 1 participant is low-vision or colorblind
- Mix of iOS and Android users (minimum 2 of each)

## Nice to Have

- Range of service eras (post-9/11, Gulf War, Vietnam-era)
- Mix of disability ratings (0-30%, 40-70%, 80-100%)
- At least 1 participant in a rural area with limited connectivity
- Range of device ages (include at least 1 participant on a phone 3+ years old)
- At least 1 participant who has called the VA help desk about a navigation issue
- Mix of tech comfort levels (not all power users)

## Exclusions

- VA employees or contractors
- UX designers, researchers, or software developers
- Participated in a VA mobile app study in the past 6 months
- Works for or consults with a VA vendor or digital services contractor
