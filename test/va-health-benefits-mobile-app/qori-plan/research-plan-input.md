### Product / feature area *
```
VA Mobile App - main navigation structure, information architecture, search functionality, and task completion flows across health and benefits features
```

### Study title *
```
VA Health and Benefits Mobile App Navigation Research
```

### What decision will this research inform? *
```
This research will inform the product team's decision on which navigation improvements to prioritize for the mobile app redesign scheduled for Q1 2026. Findings will directly influence decisions about main menu restructuring, search enhancement, and information hierarchy changes.
```

### Primary research goal *
```
Identify the primary navigation and information architecture barriers that cause 45% of veterans to abandon mobile app tasks, and understand the root causes behind the current 4.2/10 user satisfaction rating.
```

### Methodology *
```
Card Sorting, Usability Testing, Competitive Analysis
```

### Target participants *
```
Primary: Veterans who actively use the VA mobile app at least 2-3 times per month
Secondary: Veterans who have downloaded but struggled to complete tasks in the mobile app
Inclusion criteria: Current smartphone users (iOS/Android), completed at least one transaction via VA mobile app
Exclusion criteria: VA employees, mobile app developers, UX professionals
Accessibility focus: Minimum 50% of participants must use assistive technologies or have documented visual/motor accessibility needs
```
