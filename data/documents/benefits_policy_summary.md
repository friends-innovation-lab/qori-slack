# Summary of VA Benefits Policy - March 2024

This document outlines changes to eligibility rules, submission processes, and updated benefits tiers.

Key Points:
- New digital form introduced for veterans with service-connected disabilities.
- Support documentation now includes DD214, income verification, and disability rating letters.
- Processing time reduced from 90 to 45 days in pilot regions.
