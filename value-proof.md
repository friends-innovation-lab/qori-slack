# 📄 CivicMind Value & Cost Savings – One Pager

_CivicMind is an AI-powered research assistant that accelerates and simplifies human-centered design workflows across federal and civic tech teams._

---

## 🚧 The Problem

Public-sector research is under constant pressure:
- Research cycles are **slow** and resource-intensive
- Transcripts, tagging, and summaries take **hours per session**
- Stakeholders often wait **weeks for digestible insights**
- Valuable findings are often **lost, duplicated, or siloed**

---

## 💡 The CivicMind Solution

CivicMind automates the heavy lifting while keeping humans in control.

| Feature | What It Does | Time Saved |
|--------|---------------|------------|
| **Transcription** | Converts audio into structured text | 60–90 min |
| **Quote Tagging** | Highlights key quotes and pain points | 1.5–2 hrs |
| **Thematic Summaries** | Extracts insights and key actions | 2–3 hrs |
| **Slack Integration** | Delivers digests to teams in real time | Days → Hours |
| **GitHub Sync** | Turns insights into trackable Issues | 15–30 min |

> ⏱️ **Estimated Time Saved per Session**: 6+ hours  
> 💸 **Cost Saved (GS-12 @ $75/hr)**: ~$450/session
