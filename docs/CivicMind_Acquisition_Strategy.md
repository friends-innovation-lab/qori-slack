
# 🧾 CivicMind Acquisition Strategy for Federal Adoption (SDVOSB Path)

This acquisition strategy outlines the optimal pathways for piloting and scaling the CivicMind Slack AI Bot within the U.S. federal government, starting with the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). The strategy is designed to align with Friends From The City’s status as a **Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB)** and focuses on leveraging federal preferences and streamlined contracting mechanisms.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Objectives

- Launch a pilot of CivicMind at the VA to validate its value and applicability
- Expand to additional VA offices through modular, repeatable mechanisms
- Enable cross-agency adoption through scalable procurement vehicles

---

## 🏁 Phase 1: VA Pilot 

### Objective
Secure an initial contract award to pilot CivicMind with a VA program office, ideally through a direct or simplified acquisition pathway.

### Recommended Path: SDVOSB Direct Award

- Use **Vets First Contracting Program (38 U.S.C. § 8127)** authority
- Pursue a **Simplified Acquisition Threshold (SAT)** contract under $250,000
- Ensure registration in **SBA VetCert (VIP Verified)**
- Target a program like VA’s Financial Management Hub or Human-Centered Design (HCD) initiatives

### Key Advantages
✅ SDVOSB preference  
✅ Rapid award timeline  
✅ Minimal competition required  
✅ Aligned with VA acquisition protocols

---

## 🛠 Phase 2: Modular VA Expansion 

### Objective
Enable CivicMind to be deployed across multiple VA offices without repeating procurement efforts.

### Recommended Path: Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA)

- Work with VA’s **Technology Acquisition Center (TAC)** or **Strategic Acquisition Center (SAC)**
- Develop BPA with modular labor categories or services:
  - CivicMind Bot Setup
  - Slack Integration & Training
  - AI Knowledge Tagging
  - Human-Centered Insight Services

### Key Advantages
✅ Scalable across VA programs  
✅ Familiar structure for VA COs  
✅ Allows task order call-downs  

---

## 🌐 Phase 3: Cross-Agency Expansion 

### Objective
Position CivicMind for government-wide use beyond the VA.

### Recommended Paths:

#### Option 1: GSA MAS Schedule

- Apply directly or team with existing MAS holder
- Pursue AI-related SINs (e.g., **SIN 132-40** for cloud/AI tools)
- Highlight CivicMind as a modular knowledge enablement service

#### Option 2: Teaming with Federal Primes

- Partner with Booz Allen, GDIT, Accenture Federal
- Leverage SDVOSB status to meet subcontracting goals
- Deliver CivicMind via primes' existing contract vehicles

#### Option 3: OTA for Innovation (non-VA)

- Use **Other Transaction Agreements (OTAs)** for R&D or pilot work
- Focus on HHS BARDA, CMS, or DoD Defense Innovation Unit (DIU)
