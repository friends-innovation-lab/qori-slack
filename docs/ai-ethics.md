# AI Ethics & Human Oversight

CivicMind is designed to support—never replace—human researchers and designers.

Our AI is assistive, not authoritative.

## 🧠 Ethical Principles

1. **Human-in-the-loop**
   All AI-generated content (summaries, insights, tickets) must be reviewed and approved by a human.

2. **Transparency**
   - AI output is labeled as such.
   - Version history logs all edits and identifies human vs. machine input.

3. **Explainability**
   - Summaries are linked to their original sources (transcripts, quotes).
   - Confidence scores and limitations are disclosed where appropriate.

4. **Fairness & Bias Mitigation**
   - Prompts are designed to reduce harm and stereotype reinforcement.
   - Feedback mechanisms allow users to flag biased outputs.

5. **Accountability**
   - All deployments must include audit logs and usage tracking.
   - Teams are encouraged to log AI use in sensitive workflows (e.g. policy, eligibility).

## 🧪 In Practice

- No AI-generated content is auto-published or pushed into code/design without approval.
- Human reviewers can edit, reject, or contextualize output before sharing.
- Error reports and bias feedback are triaged monthly.
