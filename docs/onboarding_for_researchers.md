# 🛠️ Getting Started with CivicMind (for Researchers)

CivicMind is here to help you run faster, clearer, and more ethical human-centered research in the public sector. You stay in control — the AI just helps you work smarter.

---

## 🎯 What You Can Do

- Transcribe interviews and usability tests
- Summarize past research into briefings
- Ask questions like “What did users say about form design?”
- Turn findings into actionable design tickets

---

## 🚦 How to Use It

1. Upload your transcript or research doc
2. CivicMind will:
   - Extract quotes
   - Tag themes
   - Suggest actions
3. You review, approve, and share

---

## 🤖 What AI Does — and Doesn't Do

✅ **Does:**
- Suggest themes and summaries
- Tag quotes and insights
- Generate Markdown, slides, or GitHub Issues

❌ **Doesn’t:**
- Make decisions for you
- Replace researcher review
- Automatically publish findings without consent
