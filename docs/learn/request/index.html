Research Request

Stakeholders submit research requests to your team. A structured intake form that captures context, goals, and urgency.

Type this in Slack /qori-request
🎬
GIF: Stakeholder filling out research request form

Stakeholders describe what they need, and Qori routes it to your research team

What you can do

📝

Submit Research Request

Structured form for stakeholders to describe research needs

🎯

Define Problem Statement

Clarify what problem the research should address

👥

Identify Affected Users

Specify who is impacted by the problem

⚡

Set Urgency & Timeline

Indicate priority level and any deadlines

💡

Tips for success

  • Share this command with product managers and stakeholders who need research
  • Requests are saved to GitHub for tracking and prioritization
  • Research team gets notified in Slack when new requests come in
