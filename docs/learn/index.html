How It Works

Research workflow, simplified

From request to report in four phases. Qori handles the operational overhead so you can focus on insights.

1

Plan the study

Stakeholders submit requests. AI helps draft research briefs and discussion guides.

2

Recruit participants

Track participants, generate outreach messages, and manage scheduling.

3

Run sessions

Coordinate observers, capture real-time notes, document findings as you go.

4

Share insights

AI analyzes sessions, synthesizes patterns, and generates stakeholder reports.

Why Qori

Built for how research actually works

No new tools to learn. No context switching. Everything happens where your team already works.

Lives in Slack

Slash commands mean zero learning curve. Your team is already in Slack — Qori meets them there.

Privacy first

PII redaction is built in. Sensitive data stays protected throughout the research workflow.

AI-powered

From drafting guides to analyzing sessions — AI handles the grunt work so you can focus on insights.

GitHub-backed

All artifacts stored in version-controlled repos. Full history, easy collaboration, audit trails built in.

Team-friendly

Observers can request to join sessions. Stakeholders can submit requests. Everyone stays in the loop.

Structured outputs

Consistent templates for every artifact. Reports, notes, and analyses formatted the same way every time.
