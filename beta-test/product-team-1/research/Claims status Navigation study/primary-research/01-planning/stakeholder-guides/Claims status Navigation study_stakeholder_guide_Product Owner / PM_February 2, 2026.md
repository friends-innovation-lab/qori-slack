## Interview Guide

### Opening (3-5 min)

> *Build rapport and set context*

- Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching claims status navigation and wanted to understand product priorities and strategy better.
- Everything you share helps us design better solutions. No wrong answers.
- Okay to record? [If applicable]

**Opener:** "To start, can you tell me about your role and how claims status functionality fits into your product portfolio?"

---

### Section: Priorities (35 min)

> *Understanding strategic priorities, success metrics, and decision-making framework*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | Walk me through how you prioritize features for the claims status system. | • What criteria do you use to evaluate new requests? • How do user needs factor into prioritization? • What business metrics drive these decisions? |
| 2 | What are the top 3 priorities for claims status functionality over the next 6 months? | • What's driving these priorities? • How do these connect to broader business goals? • What would success look like for each? |
| 3 | How do you currently measure success for the claims status experience? | • What metrics do you track regularly? • Which ones matter most to leadership? • What would you like to measure that you can't today? |
| 4 | Tell me about a recent decision you made regarding claims status features. | • What factors influenced that decision? • Who else was involved? • How did you weigh user needs vs. business constraints? |
| 5 | What are the biggest competing priorities that affect claims status work? | • How do you balance these trade-offs? • What gets deprioritized and why? • How do resource constraints impact your decisions? |
| 6 | How do technical constraints influence your product decisions? | • What limitations do you work within? • How do you communicate priorities to engineering? • What would you prioritize if technical debt wasn't a factor? |
| 7 | What role does user feedback play in your roadmap planning? | • How do you collect and evaluate user input? • Can you share an example of user feedback changing priorities? • What user needs are you not addressing today? |

**Listen for:** Tension between user needs and business goals, resource constraints, success metrics that might not align with user experience, competing stakeholder demands
