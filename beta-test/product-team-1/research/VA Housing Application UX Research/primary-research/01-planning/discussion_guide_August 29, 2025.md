### Warm-Up Questions — 5 Minutes

- Can you tell me about your experience with the current housing application process?
- How often do you encounter challenges or frustrations when completing a housing application?
- Have you ever used a housing application wizard before? If so, what was your experience like?

### Tasks

## Task 1: Application Completion Rate — 10 Minutes
**Objective:** Test the current housing application process to identify any pain points or barriers that may be contributing to low completion rates.

**Instructions:**
1. Go to www.testerlink.com and navigate to the housing application page.
2. Begin filling out the application form as if you were a potential tenant looking to apply for housing.

**Watch for:**
- Any areas where the participant hesitates or struggles to complete the form.
- Any confusion or frustration expressed during the process.
- Any errors or issues encountered while completing the form.

**Success Criteria:** Participant successfully completes the housing application form within the allotted time.

## Task 2: Support Call Reduction — 5 Minutes
**Objective:** Identify any areas of the application process that may lead to confusion or questions that could result in an increased number of support calls.

**Instructions:**
1. Navigate to the section of the application form where applicants are required to upload supporting documents.
2. Attempt to upload a sample document following the provided instructions.

**Watch for:**
- Any confusion or uncertainty about how to upload documents.
- Any errors encountered during the upload process.
- Any questions or concerns expressed about the document upload requirements.

**Success Criteria:** Participant successfully uploads a sample document without encountering any issues or confusion.

## Task 3: Accessibility Compliance — 7 Minutes
**Objective:** Evaluate the accessibility of the housing application process to ensure it meets compliance standards for users with disabilities.

**Instructions:**
1. Use the tab key to navigate through the application form without using a mouse.
2. Attempt to complete the form using only the keyboard for navigation and input.

**Watch for:**
- Any difficulties encountered while navigating the form using only the keyboard.
- Any areas where the participant is unable to access or interact with form elements.
- Any feedback or comments on the accessibility of the application process.

**Success Criteria:** Participant is able to successfully navigate and complete the application form using only the keyboard without encountering any accessibility barriers.

### Post-Task Interview —  Minutes

1. Can you walk me through your thought process when you encountered [specific task or challenge]? What emotions were you feeling at that moment and how did they influence your decision-making?
2. In hindsight, is there anything you would have done differently during the task? What factors influenced your choices and what improvements do you think could be made for future tasks?
3. Can you explain the reasoning behind your approach to [specific aspect of the task]? What were your priorities and how did they shape your actions?
4. How did your past experiences or background knowledge impact your performance during the task? Were there any assumptions or biases that influenced your decision-making?
5. What aspects of the task did you find most challenging or frustrating? How did you manage these difficulties and what strategies did you employ to overcome them?
6. Can you describe a moment during the task where you felt particularly engaged or motivated? What factors contributed to this feeling and how did it impact your overall performance?

### Closing — 5 Minutes

- Thank participant for their time and insights
- Confirm recording consent one final time  
- Ask about future research participation
- Provide researcher contact for follow-up questions
