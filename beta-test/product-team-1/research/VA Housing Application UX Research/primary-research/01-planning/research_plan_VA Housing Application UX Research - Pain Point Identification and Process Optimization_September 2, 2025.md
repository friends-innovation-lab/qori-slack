# 🧭 Research Plan – VA Housing Application UX Research - Pain Point Identification and Process Optimization

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "VA.gov Housing Benefits Application Portal - streamlining online application, eligibility screening, document upload, and status tracking for Veterans. Simplifies access to crucial housing benefits within the VA ecosystem, ensuring efficient and accurate processing."

**🏛️ Team/Office:** Primary Team: VA Digital Experience Team
Collaborating Office: Office of Veterans Experience (VEO)
Support: VA Housing Benefits Administration
Technical Partner: VA.gov Platform Team
Accessibility Consultant: Section 508 Compliance Office  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** Jordan Addison  
**🗓️ Date:** September 2, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

This research will inform decisions regarding the VA.gov Housing Benefits Application Portal, focusing on the online application workflow, eligibility screening, document upload system, and application status tracking. The outcome will improve user experience for Veterans, increasing application completion rates and reducing support calls. The findings will guide whether to implement a new application wizard, redesign the status tracking system, or prioritize mobile optimization.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Fast-track disability claims, starting with PACT Act claims

**Explanation:** This priority directly impacts Veterans applying for VA housing benefits by expediting the disability claims process, ensuring timely access to benefits.

**Additional Priorities (if applicable):**
- **Improve the Veteran login experience:** Enhancing the login experience will make it easier for Veterans to access the housing benefits application portal.
- **Solidify the VA.gov platform:** A stable platform is crucial for a seamless online application workflow for Veterans applying for housing benefits.

---

## 🧭 User Journey

**User Journey Connection:** The VA.gov Housing Benefits Application Portal plays a crucial role in the Putting down roots and Retiring life stages for Veterans, as it provides a streamlined process for applying for housing benefits and tracking application status.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Taking care of myself:** Completing the online application form for housing benefits
- **Putting down roots:** Uploading required documents for eligibility screening
- **Retiring:** Tracking the status of the submitted application

**Critical Pain Points (if applicable):** Veterans may face challenges in navigating the document upload system and understanding the eligibility screening process.

---

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify and prioritize the top 3 usability barriers causing the 34% application abandonment rate for veterans applying for housing benefits online, uncovering actionable insights to improve completion rates.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Pinpoint pain points in the eligibility screening process to streamline decision-making for applicants.
- Uncover issues with the document upload system to simplify the submission process for veterans.
- Identify opportunities to enhance the application status tracking feature for increased transparency and user satisfaction.

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Usability testing, interviews, and competitive analysis are optimal for identifying usability barriers and root causes behind application abandonment rates.

**Implementation Approach:**
- Usability testing and interviews will be conducted in 1-hour and 45-minute sessions, respectively
- Activities include task-based scenarios, screen sharing, and competitive analysis
- Protocols will focus on evaluating the online application workflow, eligibility screening, document upload system, and application status tracking on VA.gov Housing Benefits Application Portal

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- Collaboration Cycle requirements will be met within the specified approval timeline
- Trauma-informed research considerations will be integrated into the study
- Accessibility accommodations will be provided for participants with assistive technologies or accessibility needs.

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges:** Difficulty in finding qualified participants meeting the inclusion criteria
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Utilize multiple recruitment channels such as VA social media platforms, veteran-specific forums, and outreach to veteran organizations to ensure a diverse pool of participants.

**Execution issues:** Challenges in implementing the usability testing, interviews, and competitive analysis effectively
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Conduct thorough pilot testing of the research methods with a small group of participants to identify and address any potential issues before full-scale implementation.

**Timeline risks:** Delays in the research process impacting the standard timeline
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Develop a detailed project plan with clear milestones and deadlines, regularly monitor progress, and proactively address any obstacles to ensure timely completion of the research.

**Quality concerns:** Risks related to data validity and stakeholder satisfaction
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Mitigation:** Implement rigorous data validation processes, such as triangulation of data sources and member checking, to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the findings. Regularly communicate with stakeholders to gather feedback and make adjustments as needed to meet their expectations.
