## 🔴 Top Pain Points

**Critical - Long wait times**
Participants expressed frustration with long wait times for customer service, leading to delays in resolving issues and impacting their overall experience.

**High - Lack of personalized support**
Customers highlighted the need for more personalized support, feeling that the current level of service did not meet their individual needs.

**High - Difficulty navigating website**
Users reported challenges in navigating the website, particularly in finding specific information or completing tasks efficiently.

---

## 🟡 Key Opportunities

**High Impact - Streamline Onboarding Process**
Participants mentioned that the onboarding process for new employees could be more efficient by incorporating digital tools and automating certain tasks.

**Medium Impact - Enhance Employee Training Programs**
There is an opportunity to improve employee training programs by incorporating more interactive and hands-on activities to enhance learning and retention.
