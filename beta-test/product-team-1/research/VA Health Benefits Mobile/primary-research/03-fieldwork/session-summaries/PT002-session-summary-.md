## 🔴 Top Pain Points

**Critical - Lack of Accessibility**
Participants expressed frustration with the lack of accessibility to key resources, hindering their ability to complete tasks efficiently and effectively.

**High - Communication Breakdown**
Issues with communication breakdown were identified as a major obstacle, leading to misunderstandings and delays in project progress.

**High - Inadequate Training**
Participants highlighted the lack of adequate training as a significant barrier, impacting their confidence and ability to perform tasks accurately.

---

## 🟡 Key Opportunities

**High Impact - Streamline Checkout Process**
Customers are experiencing frustration with the current checkout process, leading to abandoned carts and lost sales.

**Medium Impact - Implement Loyalty Program**
Customers have expressed interest in a loyalty program to incentivize repeat purchases and increase customer retention.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

**Insight Type - Mental Model**
Users struggle to understand the purpose and functionality of certain features due to lack of clear labeling and guidance.

**Insight Type - Workflow Pattern**
Users tend to skip certain steps in the workflow when they find them unnecessary or confusing, leading to incomplete tasks.
