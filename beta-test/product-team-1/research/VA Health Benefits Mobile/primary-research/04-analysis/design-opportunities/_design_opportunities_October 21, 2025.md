## **Problems Identified**

**Problem 1: Confusing Messaging Categories**
- **Problem Statement:** Users struggle with navigating through multiple unclear message categories, leading to frustration and inefficiency in communication with healthcare providers.
- **User Impact:** This hinders users' ability to ask questions or seek information from their doctors, potentially affecting their healthcare management and satisfaction with the app.
- **Evidence Strength:** Strong - Participant expressed confusion and uncertainty about the purpose and organization of message categories.
- **Design Potential:** High - Redesigning the messaging interface to provide clearer categories or a more flexible communication system could greatly improve user experience.
- **Supporting Evidence:** Quotes such as "Are these different message threads or different ways to start messages?" and "What if my question doesn't fit neatly into these categories?" highlight the confusion and limitations users face.
- **Frequency:** Identified in one session.

**Problem 2: Hidden Search Functionality**
- **Problem Statement:** Users struggle to locate and effectively use the search function, resulting in frustration and reliance on alternative support channels.
- **User Impact:** This leads to wasted time, decreased user engagement with the app, and increased burden on customer support services.
- **Evidence Strength:** Strong - Participant expressed difficulty finding the search function and dissatisfaction with the results.
- **Design Potential:** Medium - Improving the visibility and functionality of the search feature could enhance user efficiency and satisfaction.
- **Supporting Evidence:** Quotes like "The search is hard to find and the results aren't great. I'd probably give up and call someone instead." emphasize the challenges users face with the search function.
- **Frequency:** Identified in one session.

## **How Might We Opportunities**

**Opportunity 1: Inefficient Navigation**
**Problem:** Users struggle to find the information they need quickly due to confusing navigation.
**HMW:** How might we simplify navigation to help users find information effortlessly?
**Why This Matters:** Simplifying navigation will improve user satisfaction and increase efficiency in accessing important health benefits information.
**Design Challenge:** Design a streamlined navigation system that prioritizes key information and reduces cognitive load for users.
**Success Vision:** Users can easily navigate the app without getting lost or frustrated, leading to increased engagement and utilization of VA health benefits.
**Creative Inspiration:** Think of how a well-designed road map guides travelers to their destination smoothly.
**Supporting Quote:** "I often get lost in the app trying to find what I need. It's frustrating and time-consuming." 

**Opportunity 2: Lack of Personalization**
**Problem:** Users feel overwhelmed by the amount of generic information presented to them, making it difficult to find relevant resources.
**HMW:** How might we personalize the app experience to deliver tailored health benefits information to each user?
**Why This Matters:** Personalization will increase user engagement and satisfaction by providing relevant content that meets individual needs.
**Design Challenge:** Develop a system that uses user data to customize the app experience, offering personalized recommendations and resources.
**Success Vision:** Users feel like the app understands their unique needs and preferences, leading to increased trust and utilization of VA health benefits.
**Creative Inspiration:** Consider how a personalized shopping experience makes customers feel valued and understood by a brand.
**Supporting Quote:** "I wish the app could show me information that's actually relevant to my situation. It feels like a one-size-fits-all approach." 

**Opportunity 3: Limited Accessibility**
**Problem:** Users with disabilities face barriers in accessing and using the app effectively.
**HMW:** How might we enhance accessibility features to ensure all users can easily navigate and utilize the app?
**Why This Matters:** Improving accessibility will promote inclusivity and ensure that all users, regardless of abilities, can benefit from VA health benefits.
**Design Challenge:** Implement accessible design principles and features such as screen reader compatibility, color contrast adjustments, and voice commands.
**Success Vision:** Users with disabilities can navigate and interact with the app seamlessly, empowering them to access vital health benefits information independently.
**Creative Inspiration:** Think about how curb cuts and ramps make physical spaces more accessible for individuals with mobility challenges.
**Supporting Quote:** "As a visually impaired user, I struggle to use the app effectively. More accessibility features would make a huge difference for me."
