## 🧠 Detailed Findings & Recommendations

## Finding 1: Difficulty in Accessing Important Features

**What we observed:** Participants struggled to locate and access key features within the app, such as the search function and settings menu. This led to frustration and decreased efficiency in completing tasks.

**Supporting evidence:**
> "I spent a lot of time trying to find where the search bar was located. It should be more obvious." - P1
> "I didn't even know there was a settings menu until someone pointed it out to me. It's not intuitive." - P2
> "I had to ask for help to figure out how to change my profile picture. It shouldn't be that hard." - P3

**Behavioral indicators:** Participants spent an average of 2 minutes searching for the search function and 3 minutes trying to locate the settings menu during usability testing sessions.

**Frequency:** 8 out of 10 participants experienced difficulty in accessing important features.

**Recommendations:**
• Increase visibility of key features by placing them in prominent locations.
• Provide clear labels and icons for essential functions to aid discoverability.
• Implement a guided tutorial or onboarding process to educate users on the app's features.

**Impact:** Improving the accessibility of important features will enhance user efficiency, satisfaction, and overall success in achieving their goals within the app.

## Finding 2: Confusion with Error Messages

**What we observed:** Participants encountered error messages while using the app, but found them to be vague and unhelpful in guiding them towards a solution. This led to confusion and frustration.

**Supporting evidence:**
> "When I got that error message, I had no idea what it meant or what I was supposed to do next." - P4
> "The error pop-up just said 'Oops, something went wrong.' Well, that's not very helpful, is it?" - P5
> "I kept getting the same error message over and over, but it didn't tell me how to fix it. It was really annoying." - P6

**Behavioral indicators:** Participants spent an average of 5 minutes attempting to resolve issues caused by unclear error messages during usability testing sessions.

**Frequency:** 7 out of 10 participants experienced confusion with error messages.

**Recommendations:**
• Provide specific and actionable instructions in error messages to guide users towards resolving issues.
• Include links to relevant help articles or support resources within error messages for further assistance.
• Allow users to report errors directly from the app to facilitate quicker resolution.

**Impact:** Clear and informative error messages will help users troubleshoot issues effectively, reducing frustration and improving their overall experience and success with the app.
