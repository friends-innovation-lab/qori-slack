## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Interviews are the most suitable method for gathering in-depth insights and personal experiences related to the phrase "Hello how are you." Through interviews, we can explore the nuances and variations in how individuals interpret and respond to this common greeting.

**Implementation Approach:**
- **Session Format:** One-on-one semi-structured interviews lasting approximately 30-45 minutes to allow for detailed discussions.
- **Key Activities:** Utilizing open-ended questions to explore participants' thoughts, feelings, and behaviors when encountering the phrase "Hello how are you."
- **Evaluation Protocol:** Analyzing interview transcripts for common themes and patterns, as well as conducting member checking to ensure the accuracy of data interpretation.

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- **Collaboration Cycle:** Coordinating with VA staff to schedule interviews with veteran participants within the specified timeline.
- **Trauma-Informed Approach:** Sensitivity to potential triggers and providing a safe space for veterans to share their experiences.
- **Accessibility:** Offering options for virtual interviews, providing closed captioning for participants with hearing impairments, and ensuring all materials are available in accessible formats.
