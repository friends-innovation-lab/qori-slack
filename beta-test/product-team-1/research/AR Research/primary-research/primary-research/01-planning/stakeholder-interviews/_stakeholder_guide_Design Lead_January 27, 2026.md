## Interview Guide

### Opening (3-5 min)

> *Build rapport and set context*

- Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching AR features and wanted to understand the design constraints and processes better.
- Everything you share helps us design better solutions. No wrong answers.
- Okay to record? [If applicable]

**Opener:** "To start, can you tell me about your role as Design Lead and how you approach AR feature design on the mobile team?"

---

### Section 1: Design Constraints (15 min)

> *Understand technical, policy, and resource limitations affecting AR design*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What are the biggest technical constraints you face when designing AR features? | • How do device limitations affect your design decisions? • What performance thresholds do you have to design around? |
| 2 | How do policy or compliance requirements shape your AR design approach? | • What can't you do that you wish you could? • How do privacy concerns influence AR feature design? |
| 3 | What resource constraints most impact your AR design work? | • How does timeline pressure affect design quality? • What tools or team capacity would change your approach? |

**Listen for:** Specific technical limitations, regulatory boundaries, team capacity issues

---

### Section 2: Design Processes (15 min)

> *Understand how AR features move from concept to implementation*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | Walk me through how an AR feature goes from initial concept to final implementation. | • Where do bottlenecks typically occur? • How do you validate design decisions along the way? |
| 2 | How do you collaborate with engineers during AR feature development? | • What gets lost in translation between design and development? • How do technical constraints surface during the process? |

**Listen for:** Process friction points, collaboration challenges, decision-making bottlenecks

---

### Section 3: Connecting to User Research (10 min)

> *Share relevant user findings and get stakeholder perspective*

"In our user research, we're exploring AR adoption and usage patterns. From your design perspective..."

| Finding | Question |
|---------|----------|
| Users may struggle with AR feature discoverability | "What design constraints affect how prominently you can surface AR features?" |
| Performance issues could impact user adoption | "What technical limitations do you have to design around that users might notice?" |

---

### Closing (5 min)

- "What design challenges around AR haven't I asked about that you think I should understand?"
- "Who else should I talk to to better understand the technical or policy constraints?"
- "Can I follow up if I have more questions as the research progresses?"

**Thank them for their time.**
