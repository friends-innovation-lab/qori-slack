## **Key Obstacles**

**Job: "Check payment history"**

🚧 **Current Obstacles:** 
1. Slow loading times impacting users requiring predictable interfaces.
2. Lack of progress indicators during loading states.
3. Insufficient feedback about system limitations when no active claims are found.
4. Inconsistent text sizing affecting accessibility.
5. No timeout management for users requiring additional processing time.

🏛️ **System Barriers:** 
1. Connectivity issues in rural areas compounding accessibility challenges.
2. Limited data plans restricting usage of accessibility features.
3. Older devices common in rural areas not supporting latest accessibility APIs.
4. Lack of backup support options when digital accessibility fails.

❓ **Information Gaps:** 
1. Users may not understand the reasons behind system limitations.
2. Lack of clear communication about loading times and system processes.

😟 **Emotional Barriers:** 
1. Frustration due to slow loading times and lack of feedback.
2. Patience tested by a combination of performance issues and accommodation gaps.
3. User may feel overwhelmed by the lack of clear communication and system limitations.

⚡ **Resource Constraints:** 
1. Limited technical support options in rural areas.
2. Data plan constraints may limit usage of accessibility features requiring additional bandwidth.

🎯 **Primary Blocker:** 
The biggest single obstacle preventing job success is the lack of progress indicators and feedback during loading states, impacting users' ability to complete the task efficiently.

📊 **Impact Severity:** 
High - The obstacles significantly affect the user's ability to check payment history and create frustration and inefficiencies in the process.

💬 **Evidence:** 
1. *"I have patience, but this app tests it."*
2. *"Loading states provide no programmatic information for assistive technologies."*
3. *"No active claims" insufficient for user understanding - violates WCAG 3.3.3."*

## **Opportunities**

**Job: Impact Assessment**

💡 **Primary Opportunity:** Develop a standardized impact assessment framework to ensure consistency and accuracy in evaluating the effects of interventions.

🛠️ **Implementation Approach:**
- **Immediate action:** Establish a cross-functional team to create the framework within 3 months.
- **System change:** Integrate the impact assessment framework into existing project management tools for easy access and adoption.
- **Resource requirement:** Training for staff on how to use the framework effectively.

⚡ **Quick Wins:** 
- Conduct a training session on impact assessment for all staff.
- Create templates for impact assessment reports to streamline the process.

🚀 **Long-term Solutions:** 
- Implement automated data collection tools to simplify impact assessment processes.
- Develop a database for storing and analyzing impact assessment data for future reference.

📊 **Success Indicators:** 
- Increased consistency in impact assessment results.
- Reduction in time spent on impact assessment tasks.

🎯 **Team Ownership:** 
- Project management team responsible for implementing the impact assessment framework.
- Data analysis team responsible for developing data collection tools.

🎯 **Team Ownership:**
