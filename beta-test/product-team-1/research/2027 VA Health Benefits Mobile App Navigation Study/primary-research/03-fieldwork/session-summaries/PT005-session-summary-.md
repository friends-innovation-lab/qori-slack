## 🔴 Top Pain Points

**Critical - Lack of Access to Basic Needs**
Participants expressed a critical need for access to basic needs such as food, shelter, and healthcare. Without these essentials, they are unable to meet their most basic needs for survival.

**Critical - Limited Access to Education**
Limited access to education was identified as a critical issue, with participants noting that without proper education, they are unable to secure stable employment or improve their quality of life.

**High - Lack of Mental Health Support**
Participants highlighted the high severity of the lack of mental health support, expressing that without access to mental health resources, they struggle to cope with their emotions and mental well-being.
