## 🧠 Detailed Findings & Recommendations

## Finding 1: Difficulty Navigating Dashboard

**What we observed:** Participants struggled to navigate the dashboard due to unclear labels and lack of hierarchy. They frequently mentioned feeling lost and unsure of where to find specific features.

**Supporting evidence:**
> "I couldn't figure out where to find the settings. Everything looks the same." - P1
> "I kept clicking around trying to find the report section. It shouldn't be this hard." - P2
> "I wish there was a clear path to follow. It's too confusing right now." - P3

**Behavioral indicators:** Participants spent an average of 2 minutes searching for specific features, clicked on multiple tabs without success, and expressed frustration audibly.

**Frequency:** 7 out of 10 participants experienced difficulty navigating the dashboard.

**Recommendations:**
• Implement clear labels and hierarchy for easier navigation.
• Conduct user testing to validate the new design.
• Provide a guided tour or tutorial for first-time users.

**Impact:** Improving dashboard navigation will increase user efficiency, reduce frustration, and ultimately lead to higher user satisfaction and retention rates. 

## Finding 2: Lack of Customization Options

**What we observed:** Users expressed a desire for more customization options to tailor the dashboard to their specific needs. They mentioned feeling limited by the preset configurations and layouts.

**Supporting evidence:**
> "I wish I could rearrange the widgets on the dashboard. It would make it more useful for me." - P4
> "I want to be able to choose which metrics to display. Right now, it's one-size-fits-all." - P5
> "Customization is key for me. I need to see what's important to my workflow." - P6

**Behavioral indicators:** Participants attempted to drag and drop widgets, clicked on settings options looking for customization features, and expressed disappointment when unable to personalize the dashboard.

**Frequency:** 8 out of 10 participants expressed a desire for more customization options.

**Recommendations:**
• Implement drag-and-drop functionality for widget customization.
• Allow users to choose which metrics to display on the dashboard.
• Provide a settings menu for users to personalize their dashboard layout.

**Impact:** By offering more customization options, users will feel empowered to tailor the dashboard to their unique needs, leading to increased engagement, improved user experience, and higher retention rates.
