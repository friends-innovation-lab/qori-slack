## 🧠 Detailed Findings & Recommendations

## Finding 1: Difficulty Navigating Menu Options

**What we observed:** Participants struggled to navigate through the menu options, often taking longer than expected to find the desired feature. They expressed frustration with the lack of clear labels and organization.

**Supporting evidence:**
> "I kept clicking around trying to find where I needed to go, it was so confusing." - P1
> "I had to backtrack a few times because I couldn't remember where I saw that option." - P2
> "The menu layout doesn't make sense to me, I wish it was more intuitive." - P3

**Behavioral indicators:** Participants clicked on multiple menu items before finding the right one, hesitated when selecting options, and expressed confusion verbally during the task.

**Frequency:** 8 out of 10 participants experienced difficulty navigating menu options.

**Recommendations:**
• Reorganize menu items based on user tasks and frequency of use.
• Use clear and concise labels for each menu option.
• Implement a search function within the menu for quick access to features.

**Impact:** Improving menu navigation will enhance user efficiency, reduce frustration, and increase overall satisfaction with the platform.

## Finding 2: Lack of Feedback on Form Submission

**What we observed:** After submitting a form, participants were unsure if their action was successful due to the lack of immediate feedback. This led to uncertainty and doubt about whether their information was properly recorded.

**Supporting evidence:**
> "I clicked submit, but nothing happened. Did it go through?" - P4
> "I wish there was a message confirming that my form was submitted successfully." - P5
> "I had to refresh the page to see if my changes were saved, it would be nice to have a notification." - P6

**Behavioral indicators:** Participants hesitated after submitting a form, looked for visual cues indicating success, and inquired about the status of their submission.

**Frequency:** 7 out of 10 participants expressed concern about the lack of feedback on form submission.

**Recommendations:**
• Implement a visual confirmation message upon successful form submission.
• Provide an audible notification or change in button state to indicate completion.
• Display a loading animation to reassure users that their action is being processed.

**Impact:** Providing immediate feedback on form submission will increase user confidence, reduce uncertainty, and improve the overall user experience on the platform.
