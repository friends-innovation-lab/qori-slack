## 🎯 Business Context

The development of a comprehensive database tailored specifically for Veterans Affairs (VA) and addressing the unique challenges faced by veterans is essential for improving healthcare services and support. This study aims to create a new database system that will streamline information management, enhance communication among healthcare providers, and ultimately improve the quality of care for our nation's veterans. By focusing on VA-specific challenges, this research seeks to address the gaps in current database systems and provide a more efficient and effective solution for meeting the healthcare needs of our veterans.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Limited access to updated medical records in the new database is causing delays in treatment for Veterans
- Inaccurate data migration from the old system to the new database is leading to errors in patient care and service delivery
- Difficulty navigating the interface of the new database is hindering efficient data entry and retrieval for VA staff

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- "Where veterans encounter challenges when inputting information into the new database system."
- "Which features of the new database system are most critical for veterans to effectively navigate and utilize."
- "Why veterans may experience difficulties accessing and retrieving data from the new database system."
- "How veterans currently interact with the new database system and any pain points they encounter during this process."

## 🎯 Target Barriers

- Users are unable to complete registration due to unclear error messages and lack of guidance on required fields.
- Users experience slow loading times when navigating between pages, leading to frustration and potential abandonment.
- Users struggle to find key features on the interface due to cluttered design and lack of visual hierarchy.
- Users encounter bugs and glitches when trying to submit forms or interact with interactive elements on the platform.
- Users are overwhelmed by the amount of information presented on the dashboard, making it difficult to focus on important tasks.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. "What barriers prevent veterans from effectively navigating the new DB interface?"
2. "Why do veterans struggle with finding specific information within the new DB system?"
3. "What causes veterans to encounter difficulties in accessing and utilizing key features of the new DB platform?"
4. "How do veterans currently experience challenges in adapting to the new DB layout and functionality?"

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
1. The lack of user-friendly interface in the new DB increases the time required for users to complete tasks.
2. Veterans with PTSD face more difficulties navigating the new DB due to complex language and unclear instructions.
3. The current design of the new DB leads to a higher rate of errors and inaccuracies in data entry.
