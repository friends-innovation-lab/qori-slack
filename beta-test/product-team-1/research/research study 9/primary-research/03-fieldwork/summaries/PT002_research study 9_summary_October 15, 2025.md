# 📝 Session Summary - PT002

## ⚠️ Top 3 Pain Points

**Accessibility:** Unlabeled buttons causing confusion and frustration for users, leading to significant accessibility barriers and potential legal risks.

**Market Exclusion:** Inadequate accessibility measures resulting in exclusion of a substantial market segment (26% of US adults with disabilities), leading to missed revenue opportunities and negative brand perception.

**Competitive Advantage:** Successful implementation of accessibility features in certain sections demonstrating competitive advantage potential and user loyalty, highlighting the importance of proper accessibility execution for business success.

🔸 Significant time increases were noted for tasks with accessibility barriers, highlighting the impact of poor design on user efficiency and satisfaction.

🔸 Successful completion of tasks with proper accessibility implementation led to positive user reactions, demonstrating the value of investing in accessibility for competitive advantage.
