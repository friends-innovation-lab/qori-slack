## 🎯 Key Themes

### [Usability Challenges for Older Veterans in Telehealth]

**What the documents show:** 
Older Veterans faced usability challenges with telehealth platforms, including difficulty in joining appointments, technical issues, and the need for human support options.

**Evidence from documents:**
- "Current VVC interface presents significant usability barriers for older Veterans." - from JMIR_Older_Veterans_Telehealth_Usability_2024.md.pdf
- "Many participants exhibited 'click hesitancy,' pausing extensively before interactions due to fear of negative consequences." - from JMIR_Older_Veterans_Telehealth_Usability_2024.md.pdf

**Why it matters for 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App:**
Understanding the specific usability challenges faced by older Veterans will be crucial in designing a user-friendly mobile app that addresses their needs effectively.

### [Technical Issues and Access Challenges in Telehealth]

**What the documents show:** 
Veterans reported technical difficulties in joining telehealth appointments, including audio/video problems, browser compatibility issues, and network connectivity challenges.

**Evidence from documents:**
- "34% of Veterans reported difficulty joining video appointments on their first attempt." - from VA_OIG_Telehealth_Access_Report_2024.md.pdf
- "Most common issues reported: Audio problems (can't hear or be heard), Video not working, Couldn't figure out how to join." - from VFW_Telehealth_Survey_Report_2024.md.pdf

**Why it matters for 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App:**
Addressing technical issues and improving access to telehealth services through a mobile app will be essential for enhancing the overall user experience and ensuring seamless connectivity.

### [Importance of Pre-Appointment Preparation and Testing]

**What the documents show:** 
Veterans highlighted the need for clear instructions and tools to test their setup before telehealth appointments, leading to improved experiences and successful connections.

**Evidence from documents:**
- "Only 34% of respondents said they received clear instructions on how to prepare for their telehealth appointment." - from VFW_Telehealth_Survey_Report_2024.md.pdf
- "Respondents who tested their setup beforehand were 3x more likely to rate their telehealth experience as 'excellent'." - from VFW_Telehealth_Survey_Report_2024.md.pdf

**Why it matters for 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App:**
Implementing pre-appointment testing features in the mobile app can help Veterans ensure their devices are ready for telehealth sessions, leading to smoother and more successful interactions.

### [Need for Caregiver-Friendly Features in Telehealth]

**What the documents show:** 
A significant percentage of Veterans required assistance from caregivers to connect to telehealth appointments, indicating the importance of caregiver-friendly features in telehealth platforms.

**Evidence from documents:**
- "44% of respondents said they needed help from a family member, caregiver, or friend to connect to their telehealth appointment." - from VFW_Telehealth_Survey_Report_2024.md.pdf
- "Create features that allow caregivers to help set up appointments while maintaining Veteran privacy for the clinical conversation." - from VFW_Telehealth_Survey_Report_2024.md.pdf

**Why it matters for 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App:**
Including features that facilitate caregiver involvement in the mobile app design can enhance accessibility and usability for Veterans who rely on assistance for their telehealth interactions.

### [Focus on Accessibility and Inclusivity in Telehealth Platforms]

**What the documents show:** 
There is a need to prioritize accessibility features in telehealth platforms to meet the needs of Veterans with visual, hearing, cognitive, and motor impairments.

**Evidence from documents:**
- "Ensure telehealth platforms meet the needs of Veterans with visual, hearing, cognitive, and motor impairments." - from VFW_Telehealth_Survey_Report_2024.md.pdf
- "Involve disabled Veterans in testing and design." - from VFW_Telehealth_Survey_Report_2024.md.pdf

**Why it matters for 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App:**
By focusing on accessibility and inclusivity in the mobile app design, the VA can ensure that all Veterans, regardless of their abilities, can effectively utilize and benefit from telehealth services.
