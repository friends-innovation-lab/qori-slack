## **Jobs Identified**

1. **Job [1]: "Efficiently Access Benefits Information"**
- **Core Motivation:** To quickly and easily access information about benefits, including claim status and disability ratings.
- **Triggering Context:** Needing to check the status of a filed claim or understand disability ratings.
- **User Types:** Veterans like PT003 and PT002 who rely on the VA app for benefits information.
- **Job Scope:** Broad, covering the need to navigate benefits sections, check claim status, and understand disability ratings.
- **Supporting Evidence:** Quotes such as "No active claims" showing even though a claim was filed and "This just shows '40%' but doesn't tell me what conditions that includes" indicate the struggle to access benefits information.
- **Distinctiveness:** This job focuses specifically on the need to efficiently access and understand benefits information, differentiating it from other jobs related to app navigation or profile management.

2. **Job [2]: "Navigate App for Profile Management"**
- **Core Motivation:** To easily manage and update personal profile information within the VA app.
- **Triggering Context:** Needing to edit contact details, update phone numbers, or make changes to personal information.
- **User Types:** Veterans like PT003 and PT001 who engage with the app for profile management purposes.
- **Job Scope:** Specific to navigating the app for profile updates, including locating the "Edit" button and updating contact information.
- **Supporting Evidence:** Quotes like "That Edit button was hard to spot" and "It's showing my information but I don't hear any way to edit it" highlight the challenges faced in managing profile information.
- **Distinctiveness:** This job focuses on the unique task of profile management within the app, separate from broader benefits-related tasks or general app navigation.

3. **Job [3]: "Seek Assistance for App Navigation"**
- **Core Motivation:** To find external support or human assistance when facing challenges with app navigation or understanding.
- **Triggering Context:** Frustration with app loading times, unclear navigation cues, or hidden buttons.
- **User Types:** Veterans like PT003 and PT001 who prefer human interaction for resolving app-related issues.
- **Job Scope:** Involves seeking help from support channels or human assistance when the app fails to provide necessary information.
- **Supporting Evidence:** Quotes such as "By the time this app loads what I need, I could have talked to a person and gotten my answer" and "It's easier to just talk to a person" indicate the reliance on external support.
- **Distinctiveness:** This job focuses on the need for human interaction to overcome app-related challenges, distinguishing it from tasks related to app usage or profile management.

## **Key Obstacles**

**Job: Updating Profile Information**

🚧 **Current Obstacles:** 
1. Slow loading times make updating profile information feel like a time-consuming process.
2. Difficulty in locating the "Edit" button for updating profile information, especially with large text settings.
3. Unclear navigation for updating contact information, leading to frustration.
4. Lack of visibility for the "Edit" button in the Profile section, making it hard to locate.
5. Confusion around the process of updating phone numbers due to unclear navigation cues.

🏛️ **System Barriers:** 
1. App performance issues leading to slow loading times.
2. Lack of clear visual cues for important buttons like the "Edit" button.
3. Inconsistent placement of critical functions like the "Edit" button.
4. Lack of streamlined navigation for updating profile information.

❓ **Information Gaps:** 
1. Users may not know where to find the "Edit" button for updating profile information.
2. Unclear instructions or cues for updating contact information.
3. Lack of guidance on how to efficiently update phone numbers.

😟 **Emotional Barriers:** 
1. Frustration with slow loading times and complex navigation.
2. Confusion and difficulty in locating essential buttons.
3. Feeling of being lost or overwhelmed by the interface.

⚡ **Resource Constraints:** 
1. Limited data plan leading to reliance on Wi-Fi for app usage.
2. Potential lack of technical expertise or familiarity with app navigation.

🎯 **Primary Blocker:** 
The biggest single obstacle preventing job success is the slow loading times and lack of visibility for critical functions like the "Edit" button.

📊 **Impact Severity:** 
High - These obstacles significantly impact the user's ability to update profile information efficiently.

💬 **Evidence:** 
1. "Everything takes forever to load."
2. "That Edit button was hard to spot."
3. "Everything takes so long to load, it makes simple tasks feel like a big production."
4. "There's an 'Edit' button but it's way at the bottom of the page."
5. "Sometimes important buttons are hidden at the bottom of screens where they're easy to miss."
