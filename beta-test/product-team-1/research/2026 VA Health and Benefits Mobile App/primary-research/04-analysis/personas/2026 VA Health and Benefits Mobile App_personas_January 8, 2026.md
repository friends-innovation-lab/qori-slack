## Persona 1: The Assisted Navigator

**Based on:** PT001

> "Every button needs a proper label. I shouldn't have to guess what things do." — PT001, 00:02:28

**Who they are:**

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Background** | Former Navy hospital corpsman with 6 years of service |
| **Tech Setup** | Relies on VoiceOver for navigation, expert in assistive technology |
| **VA Usage** | Faces challenges with scheduling appointments, editing contact information |

**What they're trying to do:**

- Difficulty scheduling appointments — *Evidence: PT001*
- Lack of contact information editing — *Evidence: PT001*

**What blocks them:**

| Frustration | Evidence |
|-------------|----------|
| Unlabeled buttons | PT001, 00:02:28 |
| Hidden Edit button | PT001, 00:04:49 |

**How they cope:** Systematic approach to navigation, emphasis on clear labels for accessibility

**Design implication:** Improve button labeling for clarity and accessibility

---

## Persona 2: The Efficiency Seeker

**Based on:** PT002, PT003

> "That's probably the smoothest part of the app. I do this all the time and it just works." — PT002, 00:04:08

**Who they are:**

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Background** | Retired Army Sergeant First Class with 20 years of service |
| **Tech Setup** | Prefers Wi-Fi due to limited data plan, uses large text settings |
| **VA Usage** | Struggles with benefits navigation, profile information updates |

**What they're trying to do:**

- Message confusion — *Evidence: PT002*
- Benefits navigation disaster — *Evidence: PT002*
- Slow loading and frustration — *Evidence: PT003*

**What blocks them:**

| Frustration | Evidence |
|-------------|----------|
| Hidden message thread creation button | PT002, 00:01:53 |
| Lack of clear benefits section navigation | PT002, 00:02:54 |
| Slow loading times | PT003, 00:01:56 |

**How they cope:** Pragmatic approach, willingness to call for support when needed

**Design implication:** Enhance visibility of critical functions, improve loading speed

## Methodology

**Framework:** Evidence-Based Persona Development

**Approach:** Composite archetypes from behavioral patterns across participants — not 1:1 mapping.

**Data Sources:** PT001, PT002, PT003

**Why these groupings:** Identified shared patterns in frustrations and coping mechanisms across participants
