## 🔴 Top Pain Points
1. **Critical - Login Frustration**
The participant struggled to log in due to a confusing password reset process, leading to frustration and wasted time during the session.

2. **High - Missing Information**
PT003 expressed frustration when essential information about benefits was not easily accessible on the app, hindering their ability to make informed decisions.

3. **Medium - Unclear Navigation**
The participant found the app's navigation confusing, especially when trying to locate specific health resources, causing delays in finding necessary information.

## 🟡 Key Opportunities
1. **High Impact - Simplified Login Process**
There is an opportunity to streamline the login process by offering clearer instructions and easier password reset options to enhance user experience.

2. **Medium Impact - Improved Information Accessibility**
Enhancing the visibility and accessibility of benefit information within the app could significantly improve user satisfaction and decision-making processes.

## 💡 Key Insights
1. **User Expectations - Clear Communication**
Participants expect clear communication and guidance within the app, especially when dealing with sensitive information like health benefits.

2. **Behavior Pattern - Preference for Efficiency**
PT003 demonstrated a preference for efficient navigation and quick access to essential resources, indicating a need for streamlined workflows within the app.
