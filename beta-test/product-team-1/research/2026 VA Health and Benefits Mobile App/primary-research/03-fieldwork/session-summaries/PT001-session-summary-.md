## 🔴 Top Pain Points
**High - Difficulty Scheduling Appointments**
The participant struggled to find a clear way to schedule a new appointment, facing confusion with the layout and labeling of buttons in the app.

**Medium - Lack of Contact Information Editing**
In the Profile section, the participant found it challenging to edit contact information due to the lack of a clear editing option.

**Medium - Hidden Edit Button**
Locating the "Edit" button in the Profile section proved difficult for the participant as it was positioned at the bottom of the page.

**Low - Unlabeled Buttons**
The participant encountered challenges with unlabeled buttons, leading to important functions being missed due to poor visibility and lack of descriptive text.
