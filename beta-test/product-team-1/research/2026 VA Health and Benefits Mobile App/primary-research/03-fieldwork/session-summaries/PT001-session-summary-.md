In the Profile section, there was no clear way to edit contact information, leading to frustration. "It's showing my information but I don't hear any way to edit it." [00:04:20]

**High - Hidden Edit Button**
Locating the "Edit" button in the Profile section proved difficult for the participant, as it was positioned at the bottom of the page. "There's an 'Edit' button but it's way at the bottom of the page." [00:04:49]

**Low - Unlabeled Buttons**
The participant faced challenges due to unlabeled buttons, highlighting the issue of important buttons being hidden at the bottom of screens. "Sometimes important buttons are hidden at the bottom of screens where they're easy to miss." [00:05:21]

**High Impact - Clear Button Labels**
Improving the labeling of buttons throughout the app can enhance accessibility and usability for all users. The participant's frustration with unlabeled buttons emphasizes the need for clear and descriptive labels. 

**Medium Impact - Edit Button Visibility**
Enhancing the visibility and accessibility of the "Edit" button in the Profile section can streamline the editing process for users. Placing the button in a more prominent location can improve user experience.
