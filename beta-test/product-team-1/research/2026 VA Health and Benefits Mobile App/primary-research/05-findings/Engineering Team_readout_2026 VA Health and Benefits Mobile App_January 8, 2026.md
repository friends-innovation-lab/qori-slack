### TL;DR FOR ENGINEERING TEAM

### FINDINGS RELEVANT TO ENGINEERING TEAM

**Loading Speed Improvement**

> "Everything takes forever to load." - PT003

- **The Problem:** Slow loading times causing frustration
- **Severity:** High
- **Affected:** All participants
- **Suggested Fix:** Optimize app performance for faster loading

---

**Edit Button Visibility**

> "That Edit button was hard to spot." - PT003

- **The Problem:** Difficulty in locating the "Edit" button
- **Severity:** High
- **Affected:** Multiple participants
- **Suggested Fix:** Increase visibility of the "Edit" button in the Profile section

---

**Clear Navigation for Claim Status**

> "No active claims" showing even though a claim was filed. - PT003

- **The Problem:** Confusion in finding claim status
- **Severity:** High
- **Affected:** Multiple participants
- **Suggested Fix:** Provide clearer navigation for finding claim status

---

**Simplify Contact Information Update**

> "Everything takes so long to load, it makes simple tasks feel like a big production." - PT003

- **The Problem:** Complex process for updating contact information
- **Severity:** Medium
- **Affected:** Multiple participants
- **Suggested Fix:** Streamline the process for updating contact information
