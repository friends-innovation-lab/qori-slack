# TEAM READOUT
## 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App

**Date:** August 14, 2025 | **Participants:** PT001, PT002, PT003 | **Lead:** Lapedra Tolson

---

### TL;DR FOR DESIGN TEAM

- **Finding 1:** Participants struggled with unclear button labels and hidden edit buttons, impacting usability.
- **Finding 2:** Navigation within the app's Benefits section was confusing, affecting user experience.
- **Finding 3:** Search functionality was challenging to locate, leading to unsatisfactory results.

---

### FINDINGS RELEVANT TO DESIGN TEAM

**Finding 1: Button Labeling and Edit Button Visibility**

> "There's 'Upcoming' 'Past' 'Canceled' and then some buttons that just say 'Button' without any descriptive text." — PT001

- **The Problem:** Unclear button labels and hidden edit buttons.
- **Severity:** Critical/High.
- **Affected:** PT001, PT002.
- **Suggested Fix:** Improve button labeling and enhance visibility of the "Edit" button.

**Finding 2: Benefits Section Navigation**

> "This just shows '40%' but doesn't tell me what conditions that includes..." — PT002

- **The Problem:** Confusing navigation within the Benefits section.
- **Severity:** High.
- **Affected:** PT002.
- **Suggested Fix:** Enhance Benefits section to provide detailed information.

**Finding 3: Search Functionality**

> "It found some results but they're not very helpful..." — PT002

- **The Problem:** Challenging search functionality.
- **Severity:** Medium.
- **Affected:** PT002.
- **Suggested Fix:** Improve search function's visibility and relevance.
