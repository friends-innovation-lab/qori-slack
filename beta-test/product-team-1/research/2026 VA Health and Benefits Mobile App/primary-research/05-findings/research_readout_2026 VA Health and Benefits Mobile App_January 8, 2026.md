## 📌 TL;DR

- **Top Finding:** Slow loading times and frustration were common pain points.
- **Key Pattern:** Participants struggled with navigation and finding essential features.
- **Primary Recommendation:** Improve loading speed and clarify navigation elements.
- **Business Impact:** Enhancing performance and usability can lead to increased user satisfaction and engagement.

## 🔍 Key Findings

### Finding 1: Slow Loading

**What we observed:**
Participants expressed frustration with the app's slow loading times, making simple tasks challenging.

**Evidence:**
> "This is pretty typical for this app. Everything takes forever to load." — PT003

**Frequency:** All participants
**Severity:** Critical

**Recommendation:**
Improve app performance to reduce loading times.

---

### Finding 2: Navigation Confusion

Participants struggled with finding essential features and navigating the app effectively.

**Evidence:**
> "There's 'Upcoming' 'Past' 'Canceled' and then some buttons that just say 'Button' without any descriptive text." — PT001

**Frequency:** PT001, PT002
**Severity:** High

**Recommendation:**
Clarify navigation elements to reduce confusion.

---

### Finding 3: Hidden Buttons

Participants faced challenges due to unlabeled and hidden buttons, impacting usability.

**Evidence:**
> "Sometimes important buttons are hidden at the bottom of screens where they're easy to miss." — PT001

**Frequency:** PT001, PT002
**Severity:** Medium

**Recommendation:**
Enhance button visibility for improved usability.

---

- **Main Menu Structure:** Participants found the main menu structure logical and easy to navigate.
