# CONGRESSIONAL BRIEFING
## 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App

**Date:** March 13, 2026 | **Prepared by:** Lapedra Tolson | **Classification:** UNCLASSIFIED

---

### BOTTOM LINE UP FRONT

- **Issue:** Navigation challenges impacting user satisfaction.
- **Impact:** 45% of veterans abandoning tasks due to navigation issues.
- **Status:** Research conducted to address main navigation and information architecture barriers.

---

### BACKGROUND

- **Study Type:** Usability testing, interviews, competitive analysis.
- **Participants:** Veterans with diverse needs and experiences.
- **Focus:** Improving main navigation structure, information architecture, search functionality.

---

### KEY FINDINGS

**Finding 1: Slow Loading and Frustration**
- **Veteran Impact:** Frustration with slow loading times.
- **Evidence:** "Everything takes forever to load." — PT003
- **Status:** Requires immediate action to improve app performance.

**Finding 2: Message Confusion**
- **Veteran Impact:** Difficulty in starting new message threads.
- **Evidence:** "I see there's a compose button at the bottom..." — PT002
- **Status:** Clearer message thread creation process needed.

**Finding 3: Difficulty Scheduling Appointments**
- **Veteran Impact:** Confusion with scheduling new appointments.
- **Evidence:** "Buttons that just say 'Button' without any descriptive text." — PT001
- **Status:** Addressing button labeling for clarity.
