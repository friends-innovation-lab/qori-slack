# TEAM READOUT
## 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App

**Date:** August 14, 2025 | **Participants:** PT001, PT002, PT003 | **Lead:** Lapedra Tolson

---

### TL;DR FOR ACCESSIBILITY TEAM

- **Finding 1:** Participants highlighted the need for clearer button labels and improved navigation.
- **Finding 2:** Challenges with editing profile information and locating important buttons.
- **Finding 3:** Opportunities to enhance loading speed and streamline user tasks.

---

### FINDINGS RELEVANT TO ACCESSIBILITY TEAM

**Finding 1: Clear Button Labels**

> "There's 'Upcoming' 'Past' 'Canceled' and then some buttons that just say 'Button' without any descriptive text." — PT001

- **The Problem:** Unlabeled buttons impacting user experience.
- **Severity:** Critical
- **Affected:** PT001
- **Suggested Fix:** Improve button labeling for clarity and accessibility.

**Finding 2: Edit Button Visibility**

> "That Edit button was hard to spot." — PT002

- **The Problem:** Difficulty in locating the "Edit" button for profile updates.
- **Severity:** High
- **Affected:** PT002
- **Suggested Fix:** Enhance the visibility of the "Edit" button in the Profile section.

**Finding 3: Loading Speed Improvement**

> "Everything takes forever to load." — PT003

- **The Problem:** Slow loading times causing frustration.
- **Severity:** High
- **Affected:** PT003
- **Suggested Fix:** Improve app performance to reduce loading times.

### IMPLEMENTATION CHECKLIST

- [ ] Implement measures to improve loading speed.
