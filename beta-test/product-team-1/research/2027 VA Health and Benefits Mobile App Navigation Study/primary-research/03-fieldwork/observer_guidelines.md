# 👀 Observer Guidelines — VA Research Sessions

**Your observations help us build better experiences for Veterans.** This guide covers what you need to know.

---

## 🔒 Before Anything Else

- **All participant information is confidential** — don't discuss outside the research team
- **Stay muted/silent** throughout the session
- **Let the researcher handle** any issues that arise

---

## ⚙️ Setup Checklist

| Remote Sessions | In-Person Sessions |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| ☐ Test audio/video 15 min early | ☐ Arrive 15 min early |
| ☐ Close apps (no notifications) | ☐ Silence all devices |
| ☐ Use headphones | ☐ Sit where you can observe without distracting |
| ☐ Mute yourself upon joining | ☐ Bring notepad + backup pen |

---

## 🎯 Your Role

### 📝 Note-Taker

**Focus:** Capture what happens

| Do This | Deliver Within 1 Hour |
|---------|----------------------|
| Write exact quotes | Raw notes shared with team |
| Note reactions: confusion, frustration, delight | Top 3 key quotes highlighted |
| Timestamp key moments | Any technical issues flagged |
| Track task success/failure | |

**Tip:** Don't interpret — just document. Use shorthand. Sync with recording later.

---

### 👁️ Silent Observer

**Focus:** Feel the experience

| Do This | Deliver Within 1 Hour |
|---------|----------------------|
| Watch the emotional journey | 2-3 key insights |
| Notice what's NOT said | Overall experience assessment |
| Pay attention to micro-expressions | Questions for follow-up |
| Think big picture | |

**Tip:** No visible notes. Trust your instincts. Focus on transitions and friction points.

---

### 📊 PM Observer

**Focus:** Connect to product decisions

| Do This | Deliver Within 1 Hour |
|---------|----------------------|
| Identify feature gaps | Top 3 product opportunities |
| Note business impact of pain points | Business impact assessment |
| Think: "What would we build differently?" | Follow-up research questions |
| Consider technical feasibility | |

**Tip:** Balance user needs with constraints. Look for quick wins AND long-term opportunities.

---

### 🏛️ Stakeholder Observer

**Focus:** Strategic implications

| Do This | Deliver Within 1 Hour |
|---------|----------------------|
| Observe overall sentiment | Strategic themes identified |
| Note accessibility/compliance concerns | Organizational action items |
| Think systemic changes | Resource/process needs |
| Focus on patterns across user groups | |

**Tip:** Minimal notes. Focus on themes, not details. Think policy implications.

---

## ✅ After Every Session

**Within 1 hour, Qori will prompt you to submit your observations.**

All observers:
1. Share your top 2-3 observations in debrief
2. Note anything surprising
3. Ask questions about what you saw

---

## 🚨 If Something Goes Wrong

| Issue | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Technical problems | Message lead researcher |
| Participant distress | Let researcher handle it |
| Urgent/safety issue | Follow emergency protocol |
| Unsure about something | Do nothing now, ask after |

---

## 📞 Questions?

Contact the lead researcher 24 hours before your session if you need context, materials, or a prep call.
