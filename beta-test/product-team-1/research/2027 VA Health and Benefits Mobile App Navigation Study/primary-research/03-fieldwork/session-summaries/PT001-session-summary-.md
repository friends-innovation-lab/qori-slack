## Pain Points

### 🔴 Critical

<details>
<summary><strong>Unlabeled Buttons Block Task Completion</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Prevented efficient task completion and created accessibility barriers

Multiple buttons throughout the interface were announced only as "Button" by VoiceOver, providing no indication of their function or purpose.

**Evidence:** "There's a button that just says 'Button' without any descriptive text. That's not helpful at all."

</details>

### 🟠 High

<details>
<summary><strong>Critical Interface Elements Hidden at Bottom of Screens</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Nearly missed completing tasks due to poor element placement

Important buttons like edit functions were positioned at the bottom of long pages where they were difficult to discover through screen reader navigation.

**Evidence:** Participant nearly missed edit button during contact information update task, taking 6 minutes with extensive VoiceOver navigation required.

</details>

### 🟡 Medium

<details>
<summary><strong>Appointment Scheduling Interface Lacks Clear Labels</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Created confusion and required trial-and-error navigation

The appointment scheduling section lacked clear, descriptive labels for key functions, making it difficult to locate the "Schedule new appointment" option.

**Evidence:** "I'm hearing a lot of different elements but nothing that clearly says 'Schedule new appointment.'"

</details>
