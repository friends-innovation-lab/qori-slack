## Pain Points

### 🔴 Critical

<details>
<summary><strong>Session Timeout Disrupts Task Completion</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Lost message draft and forced re-authentication during active use

Session timeout occurred twice (minutes 15 and 45), with the second instance causing loss of a secure message draft that had to be restarted. Participant expressed significant frustration about being logged out after only an hour of inactivity.

**Evidence:** "Why does this keep logging me out? I was just using it an hour ago."

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Inconsistent Text Scaling Creates Accessibility Barriers</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Reduced readability despite accessibility settings being enabled

Even with large text setting enabled, some UI elements didn't scale properly, including buttons, icons, and input fields, forcing the participant to strain to read content.

**Evidence:** "Text is still too small in some places even with the large text setting."

</details>

### 🟠 High

<details>
<summary><strong>Touch Targets Too Small for Comfortable Interaction</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Multiple miss-taps and physical strain during navigation

Observer noted 3 miss-taps during the session, with the participant moving slowly and deliberately when attempting to tap buttons due to their small size.

**Evidence:** "I need bigger buttons. These are hard to tap with my fingers."

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Confusion Between App and MyHealtheVet Integration</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Task failure and inability to locate documents

Participant couldn't distinguish between VA app functionality and MyHealtheVet, leading to confusion about where to find documents and ultimately giving up on the task.

**Evidence:** "What's the difference between 'Health' and 'My HealtheVet'? Aren't they the same?"

</details>

### 🟡 Medium

<details>
<summary><strong>Slow Loading Times Impact User Experience</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Noticeable delays that create frustration during routine tasks

Claims data took 12 seconds to load and health records took 8 seconds, which the participant noticed and commented on negatively.

**Evidence:** "The loading takes forever. I've got time, but it's frustrating."

</details>
