## Pain Points

### 🔴 Critical

<details>
<summary><strong>Claims Status Buried in Navigation</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Daily task requires excessive navigation effort

Participant checks claims daily but had to navigate through multiple menu levels and tap 5 times to reach status information.

**Evidence:** "Why is claims under Benefits and not on the main screen? I check that every day." and "I had to tap like five times to get to my claim status."

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Search Function Failure</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Complete task abandonment and user frustration

Search feature failed to return useful results for common queries, forcing participant to give up and navigate manually.

**Evidence:** "The search doesn't find what I'm looking for. I typed 'claim' and got nothing useful."

</details>

### 🟠 High

<details>
<summary><strong>Confusing Status Terminology</strong></summary>

**Impact:** User anxiety and confusion about claim progress

Technical jargon like "Evidence gathering" creates uncertainty about claim status and next steps.

**Evidence:** "I don't understand what 'Evidence gathering' means. Is that good or bad?"

</details>

### 🟡 Medium

<details>
<summary><strong>Secure Messages Location Mismatch</strong></summary>

**Impact:** Inefficient task completion and mental model confusion

Participant expected messages under Profile section but found them under Health, causing navigation delays.

**Evidence:** Took 3 minutes to find secure messages with "Expected messages under 'Profile' not 'Health'"

</details>
