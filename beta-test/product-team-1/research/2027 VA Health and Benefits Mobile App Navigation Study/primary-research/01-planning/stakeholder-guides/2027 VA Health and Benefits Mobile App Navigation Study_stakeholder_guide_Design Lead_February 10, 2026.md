## Interview Guide

### Opening (3-5 min)

> *Build rapport and set context*

- Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching mobile app navigation and wanted to understand the design perspective better.
- Everything you share helps us design better solutions. No wrong answers.
- Okay to record? [If applicable]

**Opener:** "To start, can you tell me about your role and how you approach navigation design for the VA mobile app?"

---

### Section: Design Constraints (12 min)

> *Understand technical, brand, and regulatory limitations that shape design decisions*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What are the biggest design constraints you work within when designing navigation for the VA mobile app? | • How do these constraints specifically impact navigation patterns? • Which constraints are hardest to work around? |
| 2 | Walk me through the accessibility requirements that influence your navigation design decisions. | • What specific WCAG guidelines affect navigation most? • How do you test navigation accessibility? |
| 3 | How do VA brand guidelines and design system requirements shape navigation options? | • Are there navigation patterns you can't use because of brand requirements? • How flexible is the design system for navigation components? |

**Listen for:** Technical platform limitations, accessibility compliance requirements, brand consistency needs, design system constraints

---

### Section: Design Priorities (10 min)

> *Understand what drives navigation design decisions and success metrics*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What does successful navigation look like from a design perspective? | • How do you measure navigation success? • What metrics matter most to your team? |
| 2 | When you're designing navigation, what user needs do you prioritize? | • How do you balance different user types (health vs benefits users)? • What trade-offs do you typically make? |
| 3 | How do business priorities influence your navigation design decisions? | • Are there features that must be prominently placed? • How do you handle competing stakeholder requests? |

**Listen for:** Success metrics, user prioritization frameworks, business requirement conflicts, measurement approaches

---

### Section: Design Processes (12 min)

> *Understand how navigation design decisions are made and implemented*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | Walk me through your typical process for designing or changing navigation elements. | • Who needs to approve navigation changes? • How long does the process usually take? |
| 2 | How do you collaborate with engineering when designing navigation solutions? | • What technical considerations do they raise? • How do you resolve design vs. technical conflicts? |
| 3 | How do you incorporate user feedback and research into navigation design decisions? | • What sources of user feedback do you regularly review? • How do you validate navigation design concepts? |

**Listen for:** Approval workflows, collaboration patterns, feedback integration methods, validation processes

---

### Section: Design Requirements (10 min)

> *Understand must-have vs. nice-to-have navigation requirements*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What are the non-negotiable requirements for any navigation solution you design? | • Which requirements come from regulations vs. user needs? • How do you handle conflicting requirements? |
| 2 | How do cross-platform consistency requirements affect your mobile navigation design? | • Must mobile navigation match web patterns? • Where do you have flexibility to optimize for mobile? |
| 3 | What content or features absolutely must be easily accessible through navigation? | • How do you determine what gets top-level placement? • Are there legal requirements for certain content visibility? |

**Listen for:** Regulatory requirements, platform consistency needs, content hierarchy mandates, legal compliance issues

---

### Section: Navigation History (8 min)

> *Understand past navigation decisions and lessons learned*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What navigation patterns or approaches have you tried in the past that didn't work well? | • What specifically went wrong? • What did you learn from those attempts? |
| 2 | How has the current navigation structure evolved since the app launched? | • What drove the major changes? • What stayed consistent and why? |
| 3 | What's your hypothesis about why veterans currently struggle with navigation in the app? | • What evidence supports this hypothesis? • How does this compare to other apps you've worked on? |

**Listen for:** Failed experiments, evolution drivers, current problem theories, comparative insights

---

### Closing (3-5 min)

- "What haven't I asked about navigation design that you think I should know?"
- "Who else should I talk to about navigation requirements or constraints?"
- "Can I follow up if I have more questions after reviewing your insights?"
