## 📌 BOTTOM LINE

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Critical accessibility violations and navigation barriers are preventing Veterans from completing essential health and benefits tasks, directly impacting VA's mission to serve those who served.

---

## 🎯 VETERAN IMPACT

| Finding | Veterans Affected | Severity | Mission Risk |
|:--------|:-----------------:|:--------:|:-------------|
| Accessibility violations blocking task completion | 1 of 3 | 🔴 Critical | Veterans with disabilities cannot access services |
| Claims status buried in navigation | 1 of 3 | 🔴 Critical | Daily users frustrated, may abandon digital services |
| Search functionality failure | 1 of 3 | 🟡 High | Forces inefficient manual navigation |

---

## ✅ WHAT'S WORKING

- **Logical menu structure** — Veterans understand Health, Benefits, Payments, Profile organization
- **Home screen appointments** — Immediate access to most-checked information reduces task time to 1 minute

---

## ⚡ DECISION NEEDED

| Decision | Options | Recommendation |
|:---------|:--------|:---------------|
| Accessibility compliance priority | A: Fix in current sprint / B: Plan for next quarter | **Fix immediately** — Legal compliance requirement and blocks veteran access |

## ⚠️ IF NOT ADDRESSED

- **Veterans with disabilities** will continue to be blocked from accessing essential VA services
- **Daily users** may abandon the mobile app, increasing call center volume and reducing digital adoption
- **Legal compliance risk** — WCAG violations expose VA to accessibility lawsuits
