## Theme 1: Claims Status Inadequacy

**Frequency:** 32 of 50 responses (64%)

**Sentiment:** 😤 Negative

**What respondents said:**

> "Status never changes even when things are happening" — R029

> "I check my claim status constantly but the app never tells me anything new" — R019

> "What does Preparation for Decision even mean? Give us real information." — R037

> "Claim status has said the same thing for 8 months. I've called 10 times and they tell me more than the app shows." — R044

**Pattern:** Veterans frequently check claim status but find the information vague, outdated, and less informative than calling VA directly. The most common frustration is static status messages that don't reflect actual progress.
