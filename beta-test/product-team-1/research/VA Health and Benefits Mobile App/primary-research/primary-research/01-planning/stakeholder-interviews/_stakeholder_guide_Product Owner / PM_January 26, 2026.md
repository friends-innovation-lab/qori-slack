## Interview Guide

### Opening (3-5 min)

> *Build rapport and set context*

- Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching the VA mobile app experience and wanted to understand the product constraints and decision-making process better.
- Everything you share helps us design better solutions. No wrong answers.
- Okay to record? [If applicable]

**Opener:** "To start, can you tell me about your role as Product Owner and how you prioritize features for the VA mobile app?"

---

### Section 1: Technical Constraints (12 min)

> *Understand technical limitations affecting user experience*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What are the biggest technical constraints your team faces when building mobile features? | • How do these constraints show up in the user experience? • Which constraints are hardest to work around? |
| 2 | Walk me through what happens when you want to add a new feature - what technical hurdles come up? | • Where do you typically get stuck? • What dependencies slow things down most? |
| 3 | How do backend systems and APIs affect what you can deliver on mobile? | • Which systems are most limiting? • How does this impact feature scope? |

**Listen for:** Specific technical debt, integration challenges, performance limitations, third-party dependencies

---

### Section 2: Policy and Compliance Constraints (12 min)

> *Understand regulatory and policy barriers*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What policy or compliance requirements most impact your mobile product decisions? | • How do these requirements change your feature designs? • Which are non-negotiable vs. flexible? |
| 2 | Describe the approval process for new mobile features - what gates do you go through? | • Where do features typically get delayed or rejected? • Who are the key decision makers? |
| 3 | How do privacy and security requirements shape what you can build? | • What user-requested features have you had to say no to because of policy? • How do you communicate these constraints to users? |

**Listen for:** Specific policies that limit functionality, approval bottlenecks, security vs. usability tradeoffs

---

### Section 3: Resource and Strategic Constraints (12 min)

> *Understand capacity and priority limitations*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | How do you decide what makes it onto your roadmap versus what gets deprioritized? | • What criteria do you use? • Who influences these decisions? |
| 2 | What resource constraints affect your ability to deliver features users want? | • Is it more about team capacity, budget, or expertise? • How do you work around these limitations? |
| 3 | How do competing priorities across different VA departments impact your mobile roadmap? | • Give me an example of when priorities conflicted • How do you navigate these situations? |

**Listen for:** Competing stakeholder demands, budget limitations, team capacity issues, strategic misalignment

---

### Section 4: Connecting to User Research (8 min)

> *Share relevant user findings and get stakeholder perspective*

"In our user research, we've heard some specific feedback. From your perspective..."

| Finding | Question |
|---------|----------|
| Users want features that aren't available in the app | "What's the biggest barrier to adding the features users request most?" |
| Users experience slow load times or crashes | "What's happening on the backend that contributes to performance issues?" |
| Users are confused by certain app limitations | "How do you decide what context to give users about why certain things work the way they do?" |

---

### Closing (3-5 min)

- "What constraint or challenge haven't I asked about that significantly impacts your product decisions?"
- "Who else should I talk to to better understand the technical or policy landscape?"
- "Can I follow up if I have more questions as we analyze user feedback?"

**Thank them for their time.**

## Quick Reference

| If they say... | Probe deeper with... |
|----------------|----------------------|
| "It's complicated" | "Walk me through a specific example of how this plays out" |
| "We can't do that" | "What would need to change to make it possible?" |
| "That's not my area" | "Who would know more about that decision-making process?" |
| "Users don't understand" | "How do you balance explaining constraints vs. keeping the experience simple?" |
