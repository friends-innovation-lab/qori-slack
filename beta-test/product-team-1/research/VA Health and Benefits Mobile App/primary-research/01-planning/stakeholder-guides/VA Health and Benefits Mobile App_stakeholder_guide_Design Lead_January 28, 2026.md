## Interview Guide

### Opening (3-5 min)

> *Build rapport and set context*

- Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching the VA mobile app experience and wanted to understand the design constraints and processes better.
- Everything you share helps us design better solutions. No wrong answers.
- Okay to record? [If applicable]

**Opener:** "To start, can you tell me about your role as Design Lead and how you approach designing for the VA mobile platform?"

---

### Section 1: Design Constraints (12 min)

> *Understand technical, policy, and resource limitations that impact design decisions*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What are the biggest constraints you face when designing for the VA mobile app? | • How do these constraints affect your design process? • Which constraints are hardest to work around? |
| 2 | Walk me through how policy requirements shape your design decisions. | • What happens when user needs conflict with policy requirements? • How do you navigate compliance vs. usability? |
| 3 | What technical limitations most impact the user experience you can deliver? | • How do legacy systems affect your design options? • What would you design differently with fewer technical constraints? |
| 4 | How do resource constraints affect what you can accomplish? | • What design improvements are you unable to pursue? • How do you prioritize when resources are limited? |

**Listen for:** Specific examples of constraints, workarounds they've developed, frustration points, trade-offs between compliance and usability

---

### Section 2: Design Processes and Priorities (15 min)

> *Understand how design decisions are made and what drives the roadmap*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | Walk me through your typical design process from concept to implementation. | • Where do bottlenecks typically occur? • How do you validate design decisions? |
| 2 | How do you balance competing priorities when designing new features? | • Who has input on design decisions? • How do you handle conflicting stakeholder feedback? |
| 3 | What drives your design roadmap and strategic priorities? | • How do user needs factor into roadmap decisions? • What other factors influence your priorities? |
| 4 | How do you measure success for your design work? | • What metrics matter most to leadership? • How do you track user experience improvements? |

**Listen for:** Decision-making processes, stakeholder dynamics, success metrics, strategic alignment

---

### Section 3: Connecting to User Research (10 min)

> *Share relevant user findings and get stakeholder perspective*

"In our user research, we heard several things. From your design perspective..."

| Finding | Question |
|---------|----------|
| Users struggle with navigation and finding key features | "What design constraints have shaped the current navigation structure?" |
| Veterans with disabilities report significant accessibility barriers | "What accessibility requirements do you work within, and where do you see gaps?" |
| Form completion is a major pain point, with high abandonment rates | "What backend or policy constraints affect how you can design forms and data entry?" |

---

### Closing (5 min)

- "What design challenges haven't I asked about that you think I should understand?"
- "Who else should I talk to to better understand the constraints you work within?"
- "What would need to change to significantly improve the user experience?"
- "Can I follow up if I have more questions?"

**Thank them for their time.**

## Quick Reference

| If they say... | Probe deeper with... |
|----------------|----------------------|
| "It's complicated" | "Walk me through a specific example of this complexity" |
| "We can't do that" | "What would need to change to make that design possible?" |
| "That's not my area" | "Who would know more about those requirements?" |
| "Users don't understand" | "What design constraints prevent you from making it clearer?" |
| "Policy requires it" | "Help me understand how that policy translates to design decisions" |
