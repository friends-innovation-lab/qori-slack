## Interview Guide

### Opening (3-5 min)

> *Build rapport and set context*

- Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching the VA mobile app experience and wanted to understand the policy landscape better.
- Everything you share helps us design better solutions. No wrong answers.
- Okay to record? [If applicable]

**Opener:** "To start, can you tell me about your role and how policy and compliance requirements shape what we can do in the mobile app?"

---

### Section 1: Policy Constraints (12 min)

> *Understand the regulatory landscape affecting mobile features*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What are the biggest policy constraints that affect mobile app development? | • Which policies create the most friction? • How do these differ from desktop requirements? |
| 2 | Walk me through how a new mobile feature gets policy approval. | • Where do delays typically happen? • What triggers additional review? |
| 3 | How do federal regulations like HIPAA or Section 508 specifically impact mobile design decisions? | • What's different about mobile compliance? • Are there gray areas we navigate? |

**Listen for:** Specific regulations mentioned, approval bottlenecks, mobile-specific constraints

---

### Section 2: Compliance Processes (12 min)

> *Understand operational constraints and requirements*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | How do you balance security requirements with user experience needs? | • Where do these come into tension? • How are trade-offs decided? |
| 2 | What compliance requirements affect how we handle veteran data on mobile devices? | • What can't we store locally? • How does this impact offline functionality? |
| 3 | Describe the approval process for releasing updates that touch sensitive veteran information. | • What triggers additional review? • How long do these reviews typically take? |

**Listen for:** Security vs. UX tensions, data handling restrictions, approval timelines

---

### Section 3: Connecting to User Research (10 min)

> *Share relevant user findings and get stakeholder perspective*

"In our user research, we heard several things. From your perspective..."

| Finding | Question |
|---------|----------|
| Veterans frustrated by repeated identity verification | "What policy requirements drive these verification steps? Are there ways to reduce friction while staying compliant?" |
| Users confused about why some features aren't available on mobile | "What determines which VA services can be offered through mobile? How do you communicate these limitations?" |
| Complaints about the app being "locked down" compared to other apps | "How do VA security requirements differ from commercial apps? What flexibility do we have?" |

---

### Section 4: Resource and Strategic Constraints (5 min)

> *Understand broader organizational factors*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | How do policy review resources affect the mobile roadmap? | • Where are the bottlenecks? • What would speed things up? |
| 2 | What policy changes on the horizon might impact mobile app development? | • Should we be preparing for anything? • New opportunities or restrictions? |

**Listen for:** Resource constraints, upcoming changes, strategic priorities

---

### Closing (3-5 min)

- "What haven't I asked about that you think I should know?"
- "Who else should I talk to about policy constraints or compliance?"
- "Can I follow up if I have more questions?"

**Thank them for their time.**

## Quick Reference

| If they say... | Probe deeper with... |
|----------------|----------------------|
| "It's complicated" | "Walk me through it step by step" |
| "We can't do that" | "What would need to change to make it possible?" |
| "That's not my area" | "Who would know more about that?" |
| "Users don't understand" | "What do you think causes that confusion?" |
| "Policy requires it" | "Help me understand the intent behind that requirement" |
| "Security won't allow it" | "What specific risks are they concerned about?" |
