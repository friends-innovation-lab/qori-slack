# CONGRESSIONAL BRIEFING
## VA Health and Benefits Mobile App Navigation Research

**Date:** March 17, 2026 | **Prepared by:** Lapedra Tolson, VA Digital Services | **Classification:** UNCLASSIFIED

---

### BOTTOM LINE UP FRONT

- **Issue:** Critical accessibility violations and navigation barriers in the VA Mobile App are preventing veterans from completing essential healthcare and benefits tasks
- **Impact:** Veterans with disabilities cannot access basic functions due to unlabeled buttons and hidden features, forcing reliance on phone support
- **Status:** VA Digital Services has identified specific fixes and is implementing immediate accessibility compliance measures

---

### BACKGROUND

This usability research evaluated navigation structure, information architecture, and search functionality in the VA Mobile App to address the 45% task abandonment rate and 4.2/10 satisfaction score among veterans.

- **Study Type:** In-depth user interviews with task observation
- **Participants:** 3 veterans with diverse accessibility needs and app usage patterns
- **Focus:** Main navigation structure, search functionality, and information findability

---

### KEY FINDINGS

**Finding 1: Critical Accessibility Violations**
- **Veteran Impact:** Veterans using screen readers cannot complete basic tasks due to unlabeled interface elements
- **Evidence:** "There's a button that just says 'Button' without any descriptive text. That's not helpful at all." — PT001
- **Status:** Requires immediate action for Section 508 compliance

**Finding 2: Performance Issues Drive Veterans to Phone Support**
- **Veteran Impact:** Excessive loading times force veterans to abandon digital services and call VA directly
- **Evidence:** "By the time this app loads what I need, I could have talked to a person and gotten my answer." — PT003
- **Status:** Under technical review for optimization

**Finding 3: Complex Navigation Prevents Healthcare Access**
- **Veteran Impact:** Veterans struggle to contact healthcare providers due to confusing messaging workflow
- **Evidence:** "I just want to ask my doctor a simple question, but I have to navigate through all these categories first." — PT002
- **Status:** Being addressed through navigation redesign
