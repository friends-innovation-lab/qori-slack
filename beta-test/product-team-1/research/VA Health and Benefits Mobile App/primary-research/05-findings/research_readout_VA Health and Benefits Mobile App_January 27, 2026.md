## 🔍 Detailed Findings

### Finding 1: Unlabeled Interactive Elements Block Accessibility

<table>
<tr>
<td width="70%" valign="top">

#### What We Observed

Participants encountered buttons with no descriptive text, including one that literally just said "Button," creating significant accessibility barriers. This forces users to guess functionality and prevents effective use of assistive technology.
