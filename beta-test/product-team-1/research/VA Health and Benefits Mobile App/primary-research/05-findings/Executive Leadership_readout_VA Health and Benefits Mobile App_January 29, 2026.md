## 📌 BOTTOM LINE

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Critical accessibility violations and navigation failures are blocking Veterans from completing essential health and benefits tasks, requiring immediate intervention to maintain VA's digital service mission.

---

## 🎯 VETERAN IMPACT

| Finding | Veterans Affected | Severity | Mission Risk |
|:--------|:-----------------:|:--------:|:-------------|
| Accessibility Violations | All screen reader users | 🔴 Critical | Legal compliance failure, excludes disabled Veterans |
| Search Function Failure | All participants | 🔴 Critical | Forces Veterans to phone support, increases operational costs |
| Performance Issues | Rural Veterans | 🟡 High | Digital divide widens, undermines mobile-first strategy |

---

## ✅ WHAT'S WORKING

- **Prescription Management** — "That's probably the smoothest part of the app. I do this all the time and it just works." — PT002
- **Logical Menu Structure** — Clear navigation hierarchy enables efficient task completion when properly labeled

---

## ⚡ DECISION NEEDED

| Decision | Options | Recommendation |
|:---------|:--------|:---------------|
| Accessibility Compliance | A: Gradual fixes over 6 months / B: Emergency sprint to fix critical violations | **B: Emergency sprint** — Legal risk and Veteran exclusion require immediate action |

## 💰 RESOURCE IMPLICATIONS

| Aspect | Impact |

## ⚠️ IF NOT ADDRESSED
