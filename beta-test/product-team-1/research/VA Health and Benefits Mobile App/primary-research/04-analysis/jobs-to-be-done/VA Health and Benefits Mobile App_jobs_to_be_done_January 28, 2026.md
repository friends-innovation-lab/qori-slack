## Job 1: Stay informed about my VA benefits without effort

> **When** I have claims being processed or benefit status changes,  
> **I want to** stay current on my VA benefits status without having to actively check or call,  
> **So I can** plan my life and finances with confidence.

**What this job is really about:**

| Functional Need | Emotional Need | Social Need |
|-----------------|----------------|-------------|
| Track benefit decisions and status changes over time | Feel secure that I won't miss important updates | Be seen as someone who stays on top of important matters |

**What they do today:**

> "Where would a completed claim show up?" — PT003, 20:00

Current workarounds: Calling VA support, checking multiple systems, relying on mail notifications

**Why current solutions fail:**

| Pain Point | Evidence |
|------------|----------|
| Historical claim information not accessible | PT003 couldn't locate completed claim status, system showed "No active claims" |
| Benefits information too basic for decision-making | PT002 task noted as "Partial completion: Information display too basic, lacked important details" |

**Success looks like:** I can see the complete history of my benefits and claims, understand current status, and know when changes occur without having to remember to check.

**Design opportunity:** Create a comprehensive benefits dashboard that shows both active and historical information, with proactive notifications for status changes.

**What this job is really about:**
