## 🎯 Business Context

Greetings,

This study aims to investigate the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and veterans in accessing healthcare services. By focusing on VA-specific obstacles, we seek to identify areas for improvement in delivering quality care to our nation's veterans. This research is crucial in enhancing the overall healthcare experience for those who have served our country.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Difficulty in navigating the hello platform is causing frustration and discouraging Veterans from accessing important resources and information
- Inefficient communication channels within hello are leading to delays in response times and impacting the overall effectiveness of VA services
- Lack of user-friendly design features in hello is hindering Veterans from easily accessing and utilizing the available tools and services

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans are experiencing the most frustration in navigating the hello platform.
- Which features or steps in the user flows are causing the most confusion or inefficiency for veterans.
- Why veterans are encountering barriers in using hello, such as lack of clear instructions or technical issues.
- How veterans are currently interacting with hello, including frequency of use and preferred features.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. How do veterans currently navigate the hello platform and what pain points do they encounter in the user flow?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from fully utilizing the features and resources available on hello?
3. Why do veterans experience difficulties in accessing and understanding the information provided on hello?
4. What causes veterans to disengage or feel frustrated with the hello platform, leading to potential drop-off in usage?
