### Warm-Up Questions — 5 Minutes

- Can you tell me about your experience using the VA Health and Benefits mobile app? 
- What are some common tasks you frequently try to complete on the app? 
- Have you encountered any challenges or frustrations while navigating through the app? 
- How do you typically go about finding specific features or information within the app?

### Tasks

## Task 1: Find the nearest VA health clinic — 5 Minutes
**Objective:** Test the ease of finding a specific location within the app.

**Instructions:**
1. Ask the participant to navigate to the "Find a VA Health Facility" section.
2. Instruct them to search for the nearest VA health clinic to their current location.

**Watch for:**
- Ability to locate the correct section.
- Efficiency in using the search function.
- Any confusion or hesitation during the process.

**Success criteria:** Participant successfully finds the nearest VA health clinic within 2 minutes.

## Task 2: Check eligibility for VA benefits — 5 Minutes
**Objective:** Evaluate the clarity of information provided regarding eligibility for benefits.

**Instructions:**
1. Direct the participant to the "Check Eligibility for Benefits" feature.
2. Ask them to determine if they are eligible for a specific benefit (e.g., disability compensation).

**Watch for:**
- Understanding of eligibility criteria.
- Ability to navigate through the eligibility information.
- Any frustration or difficulty in finding the necessary details.

**Success criteria:** Participant accurately identifies their eligibility status within 3 minutes.

## Task 3: Schedule a VA health appointment — 5 Minutes
**Objective:** Assess the ease of scheduling appointments through the app.

**Instructions:**
1. Guide the participant to the "Schedule a VA Health Appointment" section.
2. Request them to schedule a routine health check-up for the following week.

**Watch for:**
- Navigation through the appointment scheduling process.
- Ability to select a suitable date and time.
- Any errors or confusion encountered during scheduling.

**Success criteria:** Participant successfully schedules a health appointment within 4 minutes.

## Task 4: Update personal information — 5 Minutes
**Objective:** Test the user-friendliness of updating personal details within the app.

**Instructions:**
1. Lead the participant to the "Update Personal Information" feature.
2. Instruct them to change their address in the system.

**Watch for:**
- Ease of locating the personal information section.
- Ability to edit and save the updated information.
- Any challenges faced while making the changes.

**Success criteria:** Participant successfully updates their address within 3 minutes.

### Post-Task Interview —  Minutes

1. Can you walk me through your thought process when you encountered [specific task]? What emotions were you feeling at that time and how did they impact your decision-making?
2. What aspects of the task do you feel could have been improved or made easier for you? Were there any barriers that prevented you from completing the task efficiently?
3. Can you explain why you chose to approach the task in the way that you did? What factors influenced your decision-making process?
4. How do you think your past experiences or personal preferences influenced your approach to the task? Were there any specific habits or biases that played a role in your decision-making?
5. Reflecting on your performance during the task, what do you think you did well and what areas do you think you could have improved upon? How do you plan to address those areas in the future?
6. Can you describe a scenario where you faced a similar task in the past and how your approach differed this time? What did you learn from that previous experience that influenced your decision-making this time around?

### Closing — 5 Minutes

- Thank participant for their time and insights
- Confirm recording consent one final time  
- Ask about future research participation
- Provide researcher contact for follow-up questions
