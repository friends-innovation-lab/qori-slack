# 🧭 Research Plan – LGBTQIA+ Veterans with PTSD

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "VA Mental Health Digital Tools & Telehealth Services provide convenient access to mental health resources and therapy for Veterans. This service is crucial for improving mental well-being and ensuring timely support within the VA healthcare system."

**🏛️ Team/Office:** VA Office of Mental Health and Suicide Prevention (OMHSP)  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** Lapedra Tolson  
**🗓️ Date:** July 17, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

1. This research will inform updates to VA Mental Health Digital Tools & Telehealth Services to better serve LGBTQIA+ veterans and improve access points for a more welcoming and safe care environment.
2. This decision will enhance mental health support for LGBTQIA+ veterans, leading to increased trust in VA services and improved health outcomes, ultimately fostering a more inclusive and equitable healthcare system for all veterans.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Integrate health portal features into VA.gov
**Explanation:** By incorporating mental health digital tools and telehealth services into the VA.gov platform, veterans can easily access and utilize these resources alongside their other health information.

**Additional Priorities:**
- Improve the Veteran login experience: Enhancing the login process for veterans accessing mental health digital tools and telehealth services can streamline their experience and encourage more usage.
- Enhance Veterans' personalized online experience: Tailoring mental health resources and telehealth services to individual veterans' needs can improve their overall online experience and increase engagement.

**Project-Specific Priorities:**
- Increase accessibility and usability of mental health digital tools for veterans.
- Ensure secure and confidential telehealth services for veterans seeking mental health support.
- Implement user-friendly features for veterans to easily navigate and utilize mental health resources on VA.gov.

## 🧭 Veteran Journey

**Veteran Journey Connection:** VA Mental Health Digital Tools & Telehealth Services play a crucial role in supporting veterans in managing their mental health needs throughout various stages of their journey.

**Key Moments:**
- Taking care of myself: Recognizing and addressing mental health needs, Seeking support for an acute health event, Maintaining my health
- Putting down roots: Finding a place to live, Connecting with and serving my community
- Retiring: Managing my declining health

**Relevant "Moments that Matter" (if applicable):** VA Mental Health Digital Tools & Telehealth Services are particularly relevant in helping veterans recognize and address their mental health needs, especially during times of acute health events or when managing declining health during retirement.

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Gain insights into the unique needs, experiences, and barriers of LGBTQIA+ veterans with PTSD accessing VA mental health services, to enhance digital tools, telehealth, and provider interactions.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Identify specific challenges faced by LGBTQIA+ veterans with PTSD in utilizing VA mental health digital tools and telehealth services.
- Understand the preferences and experiences of LGBTQIA+ veterans with PTSD in their interactions with VA mental health providers.
- Gather feedback on potential improvements to VA mental health digital tools and telehealth services to better support LGBTQIA+ veterans with PTSD.

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Interviews are optimal for this research as they allow for in-depth exploration of the unique needs, experiences, and barriers faced by LGBTQIA+ veterans with PTSD accessing VA mental health services, providing valuable insights for improving digital tools, telehealth, and provider interactions.

**Implementation Approach:**
- Sessions will be conducted one-on-one with participants, lasting approximately 60-90 minutes each.
- Key activities will include asking open-ended questions to gather qualitative data on participants' experiences with VA mental health services, digital tools, and telehealth. Probing questions will be used to delve deeper into specific issues.
- Specific protocols will be developed to evaluate the effectiveness, usability, and accessibility of VA Mental Health Digital Tools & Telehealth Services, focusing on LGBTQIA+ veterans with PTSD.

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- IRB approval will be obtained prior to conducting interviews, with a timeline of approximately 4-6 weeks for approval.
- Trauma-informed research considerations will be taken into account, ensuring that participants feel safe and supported throughout the interview process.
- Accessibility accommodations, such as providing ASL interpreters or offering alternative communication methods, will be made available to participants to ensure inclusivity.

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges:** Difficulty finding LGBTQIA+ veterans with PTSD who have utilized VA mental health services
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Collaborate with LGBTQIA+ veteran organizations and support groups to help identify and recruit potential participants. Utilize social media platforms and targeted advertising to reach a wider audience.

**Execution issues:** Problems with conducting effective interviews with participants
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Provide training for interviewers on sensitive and effective communication techniques when discussing PTSD and mental health. Develop a standardized interview protocol to ensure consistency and thoroughness in data collection.

**Timeline risks:** Delays in participant recruitment or data analysis impacting project timeline
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Set clear milestones and deadlines for each phase of the project. Regularly monitor progress and adjust timelines as needed. Have contingency plans in place for potential delays.

**Quality concerns:** Data validity and stakeholder satisfaction
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Implement a rigorous data validation process to ensure accuracy and reliability of the information collected. Seek feedback from stakeholders throughout the project to address any concerns or issues promptly.
