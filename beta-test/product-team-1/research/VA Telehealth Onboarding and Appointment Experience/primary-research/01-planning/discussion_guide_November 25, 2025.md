## 🚨 Emergency Procedures

**Technical Issues:**

- Screen share troubleshooting: Check browser permissions, try different browser
- Contact technical support: [Add contact info]

**Participant Distress:**
- Pause session immediately and check comfort level
- Remind participant of voluntary participation - they can stop at any time
- Remind them there are no wrong answers
- For veterans: VA Crisis Line: 988, Press 1 (24/7 support)
- Have list of relevant support resources ready

**Data/Privacy Concerns:**
- Reassure about data handling and anonymization
- Review consent form if needed
- Stop recording if requested
