# Research Plan — VA Telehealth Onboarding and Appointment Experience

📦 **Product Area:** VA Video Connect — Allows Veterans to easily connect with their healthcare providers through secure video appointments on their mobile devices or desktop computers.

👩🏽‍🔬 **Lead Researcher:** Lapedra Tolson · VA HCD Team

🗓️ **Date:** November 25, 2025 · **Timeline:** standard

---

## 🔎 Background

This research will inform decisions on how to streamline the first-time telehealth experience for Veterans using VA Video Connect and other touchpoints. By guiding Veterans through appointment scheduling, providing timely technical checks, and coordinating messages across various platforms, the goal is to make telehealth more accessible and user-friendly for Veterans. Ultimately, this will improve the overall telehealth experience for Veterans, leading to increased utilization of telehealth services and better health outcomes. Additionally, by optimizing the telehealth process, the VA can increase efficiency, reduce administrative burden, and potentially save costs in the long run.

## 🧭 User Journey

**User Journey Connection:** VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across mobile and desktop play a crucial role in the veteran journey by providing convenient access to healthcare services and support, regardless of location.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Taking care of myself:** VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across mobile and desktop allow veterans to easily connect with healthcare providers for routine check-ups, follow-ups, and mental health support, promoting proactive self-care.
- **Putting down roots:** These telehealth tools help veterans transitioning into civilian life by ensuring continuity of care and access to VA services, even as they establish new routines and networks in their communities.
- **Retiring:** VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across mobile and desktop facilitate healthcare management for veterans in retirement, offering convenience and flexibility in accessing necessary medical services.

**Critical Pain Points:** 
- Limited access to technology or internet connectivity may hinder veterans from utilizing VA Video Connect and telehealth services effectively.
- Lack of awareness or training on how to use these digital tools may create barriers for veterans in accessing necessary healthcare remotely.

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify the specific pain points in the user journey of Veterans using VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across mobile and desktop, leading to drop-offs or technical issues, with a target of reducing drop-off rates by 20% within 3 months.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Determine the most common technical issues faced by Veterans using VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across mobile and desktop, and provide recommendations for improvement.
- Identify the key content and touchpoints that build user confidence in using VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across mobile and desktop, leading to successful telehealth visits.
- Analyze interaction patterns that contribute to user drop-offs or technical issues in VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across mobile and desktop, and propose solutions to enhance user experience.

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Usability research is specifically right for VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across mobile and desktop because it allows for the evaluation of user interactions with the platform, identifying pain points, and gathering feedback to improve the overall user experience.

**Implementation Approach:**
- **Session Format:** One-on-one usability testing sessions lasting approximately 45-60 minutes each.
- **Key Activities:** Observing participants as they navigate VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints on both mobile and desktop devices, conducting think-aloud protocols, and gathering feedback through surveys and interviews.
- **Evaluation Protocol:** Using a combination of task-based testing, heuristic evaluation, and user satisfaction surveys to assess the usability and user experience of VA Video Connect and supporting telehealth touchpoints across different platforms.

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- **Collaboration Cycle:** Ensuring compliance with VA regulations and guidelines, including obtaining necessary approvals and coordinating with VA stakeholders throughout the research process.
- **Trauma-Informed Approach:** Implementing trauma-informed practices to create a safe and supportive environment for veteran participants, including providing clear communication, respecting boundaries, and offering support resources if needed.
- **Accessibility:** Making accommodations for diverse participant needs, such as providing assistive technologies, ensuring compatibility with screen readers, and offering alternative communication methods for participants with disabilities.

**For Usability Testing:**
- **Task Scenarios:** 
1. Schedule a telehealth appointment using VA Video Connect on a mobile device.
2. Join a virtual consultation with a healthcare provider via VA Video Connect on a desktop.
3. Access and review your medical records through the telehealth touchpoints on both mobile and desktop.
4. Send a secure message to your healthcare provider using the telehealth touchpoints.

- **Think-Aloud Protocol:** Instruct participants to verbalize their thoughts, actions, and reactions as they interact with VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints.

- **Data Collection:** Set up screen recording software to capture participant interactions. Use an observation notes template to record usability issues, successes, and timing for each task. Ensure timing protocols are in place to track task completion time accurately.

- **Post-Session Interviews:** Ask participants about their overall experience using VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints. Inquire about any challenges faced, suggestions for improvement, and their satisfaction with the platform.

**For User Interviews:**
- **Interview Guide Structure:** 
- Opening: Introduce the purpose of the interview and establish rapport.
- Main Topics: Explore participants' experiences with VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints, including usability, satisfaction, and suggestions for enhancement.
- Probing Questions: Dive deeper into specific issues or feedback related to the usability of the platform.

- **Question Types:** Include behavioral questions about participants' interactions with the platform, attitudinal questions about their perceptions and feelings, and contextual questions about their usage context.

- **Recording Protocols:** Obtain consent for audio or video recording of the interviews. Ensure a secure and confidential recording setup.

- **Analysis Approach:** Conduct thematic analysis to identify patterns, insights, and recommendations from the user interviews. Use affinity mapping and a coding framework to organize and interpret the data effectively.

**For Card Sorting:**
- **Card Preparation:** Create cards representing different features, functions, or content elements of VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints. Include items like appointment scheduling, video consultation, messaging, and medical records access.

- **Sorting Methodology:** Allow participants to categorize the cards through open or closed sorting techniques, or a hybrid approach combining both methods.

- **Analysis Procedures:** Use statistical measures to analyze the card sorting results, such as calculating similarity indices, dendrograms, and similarity matrices to identify patterns and groupings.

- **Results Interpretation:** Translate the findings from the card sorting exercise into actionable recommendations for improving the organization and navigation of VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints.

**For Survey Research:**
- **Question Design:** Develop survey questions that assess usability, satisfaction, and preferences related to VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints. Use validated scales and metrics to measure user experiences effectively.

- **Sampling Strategy:** Determine target demographics for the survey and calculate an appropriate sample size to ensure statistical significance.

- **Distribution Methods:** Select suitable platforms for survey distribution, establish timing for sending out surveys, and implement reminder protocols to increase response rates.

- **Statistical Analysis:** Analyze survey responses using descriptive statistics to summarize findings, conduct significance testing to identify correlations or differences, and adhere to reporting standards for presenting results.

**For Concept Testing:**
- **Concept Presentation:** Present prototypes or mockups of new features or design enhancements for VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints. Set up scenarios that simulate real-world usage situations.

- **Testing Protocol:** Guide participants through tasks that involve interacting with the concepts, collecting feedback on usability, functionality, and appeal. Ask participants to rank their preferences among different design options.

- **Measurement Criteria:** Evaluate usability metrics, comprehension testing results, and participants' overall appeal and satisfaction with the concepts presented.

- **Iteration Framework:** Incorporate feedback from concept testing into the design and development process of VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints, ensuring continuous improvement based on user input.

**For Competitive Analysis:**
- **Evaluation Framework:** Use established heuristics and feature comparison matrices to evaluate VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints against competitors in the telehealth industry.

- **Documentation Standards:** Capture screenshots, document interaction flows, and record usability notes during the competitive analysis process.

- **Analysis Criteria:** Identify strengths and weaknesses of VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints compared to competitors, extract best practices from other platforms, and highlight areas for improvement.

- **Synthesis Approach:** Synthesize insights from the competitive analysis to generate recommendations for enhancing the usability and functionality of VA Video Connect and the telehealth touchpoints, aligning with industry standards and user expectations.
