1. Users experienced frustration with document upload failures, slow page loading, and unresponsive form elements, indicating a need for improved system performance and clearer error messaging.

3. Users appreciated features like the progress bar and auto-save functionality, suggesting that these elements positively impacted their experience and should be maintained or enhanced in future iterations.
4. The presence of emotional responses such as frustration and confusion indicates that the user experience may need to be refined to reduce negative emotions and improve overall satisfaction with the application process.
