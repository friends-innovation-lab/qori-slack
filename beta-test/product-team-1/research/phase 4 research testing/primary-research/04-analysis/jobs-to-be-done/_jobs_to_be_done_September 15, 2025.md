## **Jobs Identified**

**Job 1: "Navigating Program Selection"**
- **Core Motivation:** Understanding and selecting the correct program for housing assistance.
- **Triggering Context:** Confusion caused by unclear program descriptions and options.
- **User Types:** Veterans applying for housing assistance.

- **Job Scope:** Encompasses the entire application process, addressing technical obstacles at each step.
- **Supporting Evidence:** Quotes expressing frustration with technical issues and the impact on the application process.
- **Distinctiveness:** This job focuses on overcoming technical obstacles specifically, differentiating it from jobs related to program selection or form completion.

## **Key Obstacles**

**Job: "Uploading Documents for VA Housing Application"**

🚧 **Current Obstacles:**
1. Document upload failures without clear error messages.
2. Slow page loading causing frustration.
3. Confusing terminology leading to program selection confusion.
4. Unresponsive submit button causing uncertainty.
5. Form validation errors due to disability rating format.

🏛️ **System Barriers:**
- Lack of clear feedback on document upload failures.
- Slow server response times impacting user experience.
- Inadequate program selection guidance leading to user confusion.
- Technical issues with form validation affecting usability.

❓ **Information Gaps:**
- Users may not understand the specific requirements for document uploads.
- Users may not be clear on the differences between various housing programs.
- Users may not know how to resolve form validation errors related to disability ratings.

😟 **Emotional Barriers:**
- Frustration from repeated document upload failures.
- Uncertainty and impatience due to slow page loading.
- Confusion and indecision when selecting the appropriate program.
- Frustration with unresponsive submit button and form validation errors.

⚡ **Resource Constraints:**
- Limited technical support for users facing document upload issues.
- Insufficient guidance on program selection for users.
- Lack of clear instructions for resolving form validation errors.

🎯 **Primary Blocker:** Document upload failures without clear error messages.

📊 **Impact Severity:** High - Document upload failures significantly hinder progress in the application process.

💬 **Evidence:**
- "Upload failed."
- "Is this normal? It's been loading for like 30 seconds."
- "No clear explanation of which one I should pick."
- "I've been clicking 'submit' for like 30 seconds."
- "It says my disability rating is 'invalid'."

By addressing the specific obstacles related to document uploads, program selection clarity, and form validation errors, the VA Housing Application process can be significantly improved for users.
