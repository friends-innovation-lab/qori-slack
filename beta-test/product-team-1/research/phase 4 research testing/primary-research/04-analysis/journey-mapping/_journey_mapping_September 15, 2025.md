## **Journey Stages**

**Stage 1: "Onboarding"**
- **User Action:** New VA employees familiarize themselves with systems and processes.
- **Stage Purpose:** Introducing new employees to the testing procedures and tools.
- **Data Evidence:** Conducting usability testing to familiarize new VA employees with the systems and processes.
- **Transition:** Successful completion of onboarding leads to the next stage.

**Stage 2: "Daily Operations"**
- **User Action:** VA employees engage in day-to-day tasks using the testing procedures.
- **Stage Purpose:** Optimizing the user experience for VA employees during their daily tasks.
- **Data Evidence:** Running A/B testing to optimize the user experience for VA employees during their day-to-day tasks.
- **Transition:** Continuous engagement in daily operations leads to the next stage.

**Stage 3: "Process Improvement"**
- **User Action:** Gathering feedback through testing to identify areas for enhancement and optimization in the workflow.
- **Stage Purpose:** Identifying areas for improvement and optimization in the testing process.
- **Data Evidence:** Gathering feedback through testing to identify pain points and areas for improvement in the workflow.
- **Transition:** Implementing feedback and improvements leads to the conclusion of the user journey.

## **Stage Details & Emotions**

**Stage: "Onboarding"**

💭 **User Thought:** "I need to learn how to navigate this new system efficiently."
❤️ **Primary Emotion:** Curiosity - The user is eager to explore and understand the new system.
😤 **Pain Points:** Feeling overwhelmed by the amount of information to absorb and uncertainty about where to start.
✅ **Success Factors:** Clear and structured onboarding process, access to resources for self-learning, and guidance from experienced users or mentors.
📊 **Signal Strength:** Consistent across participants
💬 **Data Source:** Research Plan – testing

**Stage: "Daily Operations"**

💭 **User Thought:** "I hope I can complete my tasks quickly and without any issues."
❤️ **Primary Emotion:** Frustration - The user may feel frustrated if they encounter obstacles or inefficiencies in their daily tasks.
😤 **Pain Points:** Technical glitches, complex processes, and lack of user-friendly features that slow down workflow.
✅ **Success Factors:** Intuitive interface, efficient tools, clear instructions, and quick access to necessary information.
📊 **Signal Strength:** Majority reported
💬 **Data Source:** Research Plan – testing

**Stage: "Process Improvement"**

💭 **User Thought:** "I wonder if there are better ways to do this task more effectively."
❤️ **Primary Emotion:** Hopeful - The user is optimistic about the potential for improvement and eager to see positive changes.
😤 **Pain Points:** Identifying areas for enhancement, resistance to change, and uncertainty about the impact of proposed improvements.
✅ **Success Factors:** Feedback mechanisms for user input, transparent communication about changes, and clear benefits of process enhancements.
📊 **Signal Strength:** Recurring theme
💬 **Data Source:** Research Plan – testing
