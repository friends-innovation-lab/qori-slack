## Task 1: Finding Friends from City — 5 Minutes
**Objective:** To see how easily participants can find their friends from a specific city.

**Instructions:**
1. Ask the participant to search for friends from a specific city using the search function.
2. Instruct them to add at least one friend from that city to their list.

**Watch for:**
- Ease of navigating the search function
- Ability to locate friends from the specified city
- Any issues encountered during the process

**Success Criteria:**
- Participant successfully adds at least one friend from the specified city.

## Task 2: Sending a Message to a Friend from City — 3 Minutes
**Objective:** To assess how participants interact with friends from a specific city.

**Instructions:**
1. Ask the participant to send a message to the friend they added from the specified city.
2. Instruct them to include a greeting and ask about the friend's recent activities.

**Watch for:**
- Efficiency in composing and sending the message
- Use of appropriate language and tone in the message
- Any difficulties encountered while sending the message

**Success Criteria:**
- Participant successfully sends a message to the friend from the specified city with a greeting and inquiry about recent activities.

### Post-Task Interview —  Minutes

1. Can you walk me through your thought process as you completed the task? What emotions were you experiencing during the process?
2. What aspects of the task did you find most challenging or frustrating? How do you think those challenges could be addressed in the future?
3. Can you explain why you chose to approach the task in the way that you did? Were there any alternative methods you considered?
4. How did your past experiences or background knowledge influence your approach to the task? Were there any assumptions you made that affected your decision-making?
5. In hindsight, is there anything you would have done differently to improve your performance on the task? What specific changes or adjustments would you make?
6. How do you think your personal preferences or biases influenced your decision-making during the task? Are there any ways you could mitigate those biases in the future?

### Closing — 5 Minutes

- Thank participant for their time and insights
- Confirm recording consent one final time  
- Ask about future research participation
- Provide researcher contact for follow-up questions
