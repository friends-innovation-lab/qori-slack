# 🧭 Research Plan – Testing

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "Testing - Evaluating user experiences to improve VA services for Veterans. Ensures efficient and user-friendly digital platforms within the VA ecosystem."

**🏛️ Team/Office:** Testing  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** Testing  
**🗓️ Date:** September 3, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

This research will inform improvements to the Testing process, enhancing user experience for Veterans. The streamlined process will reduce wait times for appointments, leading to increased satisfaction and trust in VA services. By optimizing Testing procedures, VA can better serve Veterans' needs and improve overall efficiency in delivering healthcare services.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Fast-track disability claims, starting with PACT Act claims

**Explanation:** Streamlining the disability claims process for veterans undergoing testing will expedite their access to benefits and support.

**Additional Priorities:**
- **Improve the Veteran login experience:** Enhancing the login experience for veterans undergoing testing will ensure seamless access to necessary resources.
- **Expand the flagship mobile app:** Providing testing-related features on the mobile app will allow veterans to conveniently access important information on the go.

---

## 🧭 User Journey

**User Journey Connection:** Testing is a crucial component in ensuring the efficiency and effectiveness of the VA services provided to Veterans, VA Employees, and External Stakeholders.

**Detected Audience:** Veterans

**User Journey Connection:** Testing plays a vital role in the VA services provided to Veterans, ensuring the quality and reliability of the systems and processes they interact with.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Taking care of myself:** Testing the VA healthcare portal for accessibility and usability
- **Putting down roots:** Testing the VA home loan application process for efficiency and accuracy
- **Retiring:** Testing the VA benefits application system for ease of use and clarity

**Detected Audience:** VA Employees

**User Journey Connection:** Testing is essential in supporting VA Employees in their daily operations, ensuring that the systems they rely on function smoothly and effectively.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Onboarding:** Testing new software tools for compatibility and functionality
- **Daily operations:** Testing the case management system for data accuracy and reliability
- **Process improvement:** Testing new workflow processes for efficiency and effectiveness

**Detected Audience:** External Stakeholders

**User Journey Connection:** Testing is critical in the Professional Service Journey of External Stakeholders, ensuring that the VA systems they interact with are reliable and secure.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Client acquisition:** Testing the VA client intake process for ease of use and accuracy
- **VA system interaction:** Testing the VA portal for seamless navigation and functionality
- **Resolution and follow-up:** Testing the case management system for timely and accurate updates

**Critical Pain Points (if applicable):** Inadequate testing procedures can lead to system errors, delays in service delivery, and potential security risks for all detected audiences.

---

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify pain points and usability issues in the Testing process to inform improvements and enhance user satisfaction.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Gather feedback on current Testing procedures to pinpoint areas for optimization.
- Understand user preferences and behaviors related to Testing to tailor solutions effectively.
- Identify opportunities for innovation and differentiation in the Testing experience.

**Success Criteria:**
1. **Decision Support:** By the end of the testing research, provide clear recommendations on which features to prioritize for testing based on user feedback. Timeline: Within 2 weeks of completing interviews.

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges:** Difficulty in finding qualified Testing participants
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Expand recruitment efforts to include multiple channels such as social media, professional networks, and industry-specific forums. Offer incentives to encourage participation.

**Execution issues:** Problems with implementing interviews effectively
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Conduct thorough training sessions for interviewers to ensure consistency in questioning and data collection. Pilot test the interview process with a small group before full implementation.

**Timeline risks:** Delays that impact the standard timeline
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Mitigation:** Develop a detailed project plan with clear milestones and deadlines. Regularly monitor progress and address any potential delays promptly to stay on track.

**Quality concerns:** Potential issues with data validity and stakeholder satisfaction
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Implement a rigorous data validation process to ensure accuracy and reliability of findings. Regularly communicate with stakeholders to gather feedback and make necessary adjustments throughout the research project.
