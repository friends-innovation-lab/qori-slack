# 🧭 Research Plan – hello

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "hello - A secure messaging platform connecting Veterans with their VA healthcare providers. Improves communication efficiency and accessibility for Veterans within the VA healthcare system."

**🏛️ Team/Office:** hello  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** hello  
**🗓️ Date:** August 26, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

The research on hello will guide the decision-making process regarding its implementation. This decision will directly improve the user experience for Veterans accessing VA services, leading to increased efficiency and satisfaction. By understanding how hello can enhance the overall experience for Veterans, the VA can better meet their needs and improve overall service delivery.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Expand the flagship mobile app

**Explanation:** The target participants may benefit from a user-friendly mobile app that provides easy access to VA services and resources on-the-go.

**Additional Priorities:**
- **Solidify the VA.gov platform:** Ensuring a stable and reliable online platform for veterans to access information and services.
- **Fast-track disability claims, starting with PACT Act claims:** Streamlining the claims process for veterans seeking disability benefits.

---

## 🧭 User Journey

**Detected Audience:** Veterans

**User Journey Connection:** hello is integrated into the "Putting down roots" life stage of veterans, where they are establishing a sense of stability and community.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Putting down roots:** Exploring housing options and settling into a new environment
- **Retiring:** Transitioning from a career to retirement and seeking new opportunities for engagement
- **Aging:** Managing healthcare needs and accessing support services

**Critical Pain Points (if applicable):** Limited access to resources for housing, lack of information on retirement planning, challenges in navigating healthcare options.

---

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify key pain points and user needs related to the hello feature, informing improvements to enhance user experience.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Understand how users currently interact with the hello feature and what challenges they face.
- Gather insights on user expectations and preferences regarding the hello feature.
- Identify opportunities for innovation and differentiation in the hello feature based on user feedback.

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Interviews are optimal for gathering in-depth insights and understanding participants' experiences and perspectives on the "hello" product area.

**Implementation Approach:**
- Conduct 30-minute individual interviews with 10 participants (n=4) to explore their interactions with the "hello" feature
- Use open-ended questions to gather qualitative data on usability, satisfaction, and suggestions for improvement
- Implement VA Compliance requirements for collaboration, trauma-informed research, and accessibility accommodations for all participants.

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges:** Difficulty in finding qualified participants for the interviews
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Utilize multiple recruitment channels, such as social media, professional networks, and referrals, to ensure a diverse pool of qualified participants.

**Execution issues:** Problems with the implementation of the interviews, such as technical difficulties or scheduling conflicts
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Mitigation:** Conduct thorough testing of interview platforms and equipment prior to the interviews to identify and address any potential issues. Provide participants with clear instructions and support to minimize scheduling conflicts.

**Timeline risks:** Delays that could impact the rushed timeline of the project
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Develop a detailed project plan with clear milestones and deadlines. Regularly monitor progress and adjust resources as needed to ensure timely completion of the research.

**Quality concerns:** Risks related to data validity and stakeholder satisfaction
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Implement a rigorous data validation process, including cross-checking responses and conducting follow-up interviews if needed. Regularly communicate with stakeholders to gather feedback and address any concerns promptly.
