## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Interviews allow for in-depth exploration of participants' experiences and perspectives, providing rich qualitative data for analysis.

**Implementation Approach:**
- Conduct 1-hour semi-structured interviews with participants to gather detailed insights
- Utilize probing questions and active listening to capture nuanced responses
- Follow VA guidelines for informed consent and confidentiality throughout the research process

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- Submit collaboration request to VA stakeholders for approval before conducting interviews
- Incorporate trauma-informed practices to ensure a safe and respectful research environment
- Provide accessibility accommodations such as closed captioning or ASL interpretation for participants with disabilities.

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges:** Difficulty in finding qualified participants for interviews
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Utilize multiple recruitment channels such as social media, professional networks, and industry-specific forums to reach a diverse pool of potential participants.

**Execution issues:** Problems with the implementation of interviews, such as technical difficulties or scheduling conflicts
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Conduct thorough pilot testing of interview tools and platforms to identify and address any technical issues before the actual interviews begin.

**Timeline risks:** Delays that could impact the extended timeline of the project
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Regularly review and adjust the project timeline as needed, setting clear milestones and deadlines to ensure progress is on track.

**Quality concerns:** Potential issues with data validity or stakeholder satisfaction
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Implement rigorous data validation processes, such as triangulation of data from multiple sources, and regularly communicate with stakeholders to ensure their needs and expectations are being met throughout the research process.
