### Tasks

## Task 1: Application Process — 5 Minutes
**Objective:** To test the ease of use and clarity of the housing application process on the website.

**Instructions:**
1. Navigate to the testing URL: https://staging.va.gov/housing/apply
2. Begin the application process by selecting the appropriate options and filling out the required information.

**Watch for:**
- User's ability to navigate through the application process smoothly.
- Any confusion or hesitation during the process.
- Any errors or issues encountered while filling out the application.

**Success criteria:** User is able to complete the application process without encountering any major issues or confusion.

## Task 2: Document Upload — 5 Minutes
**Objective:** To evaluate the process of uploading required documents for the housing application.

**Instructions:**
1. Find the section on the website where users can upload documents for their housing application.
2. Select a document from your device and upload it to the website.

**Watch for:**
- User's ability to locate the document upload section easily.
- Any difficulties encountered while uploading a document.
- Any error messages or issues with the document upload process.

**Success criteria:** User is able to successfully upload a document without facing any significant challenges.

### Post-Task Interview —  Minutes

1. Can you walk me through your thought process as you completed the task? What were you feeling at different points during the process?
2. Were there any aspects of the task that you found frustrating or confusing? How do you think those challenges could be addressed in the future?
3. What motivated you to approach the task in the way that you did? Were there any specific goals or outcomes you were aiming for?
4. How do you think your past experiences or background influenced your approach to this task? Is there anything from your past that you think could have impacted your performance?
5. In hindsight, is there anything you would have done differently to improve your efficiency or effectiveness in completing the task?
6. Can you identify any areas where you felt uncertain or lacked confidence during the task? How do you think those feelings impacted your performance?

### Closing — 5 Minutes

- Thank participant for their time and insights
- Confirm recording consent one final time  
- Ask about future research participation
- Provide researcher contact for follow-up questions
