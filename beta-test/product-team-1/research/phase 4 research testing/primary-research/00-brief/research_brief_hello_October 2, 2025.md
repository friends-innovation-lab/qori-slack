## 🎯 Business Context

Greetings, 

This study aims to investigate the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and veterans in accessing mental health services. By exploring the barriers and limitations within the VA system, we seek to identify areas for improvement and enhance the overall mental health support available to our nation's veterans.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Difficulty in navigating the hello system is causing frustration and confusion for Veterans trying to access important resources and information
- Inefficient communication channels within hello are impacting the timeliness and effectiveness of VA service delivery to Veterans
- Lack of user-friendly design features in hello is hindering Veterans from easily completing tasks and accessing necessary support and benefits

- Users are unable to locate the "hello" button due to poor visual hierarchy and lack of prominent placement on the interface.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. "How do veterans currently navigate the user flows within the hello platform?"
2. "What barriers prevent veterans from effectively utilizing the features and resources offered by hello?"
3. "Why do veterans encounter difficulties in accessing and understanding the information provided through hello?"
