## 🎯 Business Context

In light of the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and the specific needs of veterans, there is a pressing need to delve into the intricacies of the support systems available to this demographic. Understanding the complexities of VA services and the barriers veterans may encounter is crucial for enhancing their overall well-being and ensuring they receive the necessary care and support. This study aims to explore the various challenges that VA and veterans encounter, shedding light on potential areas for improvement in the services provided to this population.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Difficulty in navigating through the VA website to find relevant information is causing frustration and confusion for Veterans
- Inadequate communication channels for Veterans to reach out for assistance is impacting their ability to access necessary resources and support
- Lack of mobile-friendly design on VA platforms is hindering Veterans from easily accessing information and services on-the-go
