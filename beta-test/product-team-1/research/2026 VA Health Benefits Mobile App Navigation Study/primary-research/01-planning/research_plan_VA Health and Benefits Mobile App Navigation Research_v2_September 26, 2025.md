## ðŸ› ï¸ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Usability testing, interviews, and competitive analysis are optimal for this research as they provide direct insights into user interactions, pain points, and competitive benchmarks, aligning with the goal of identifying barriers and root causes for low user satisfaction.

**Implementation Approach:**
- Hybrid sessions combining card sorting and task-based usability testing for in-depth navigation and task flow evaluation
- Interviews to gather qualitative feedback on information architecture and search functionality
- Competitive analysis to benchmark against industry standards and identify areas for improvement
- Specific protocols tailored to evaluate main navigation, information architecture, search functionality, and task completion flows within the VA Mobile App
- Collaboration Cycle requirements and approval timeline to ensure compliance with VA regulations
- Trauma-informed research considerations and accessibility accommodations for participants with visual/motor accessibility needs.
