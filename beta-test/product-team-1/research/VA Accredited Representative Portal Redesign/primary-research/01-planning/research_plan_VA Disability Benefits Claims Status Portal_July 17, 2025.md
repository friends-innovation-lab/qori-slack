# 🧭 Research Plan – VA Disability Benefits Claims Status Portal

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "VA Disability Benefits Claims Tracking and Status Portal - A platform for Veterans to monitor the progress of their disability claims. This tool provides transparency and accountability within the VA ecosystem, ensuring Veterans stay informed about their benefits."

**🏛️ Team/Office:** Veterans Benefits Administration (VBA) Digital Services  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** Lapedra Tolson  
**🗓️ Date:** July 17, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

This research will inform the decision to redesign the VA Disability Benefits Claims Tracking and Status Portal to provide clearer information on claim progress for veterans. This change will reduce confusion and frustration for veterans navigating the system, leading to improved user satisfaction and faster resolution of claims. Updating status descriptions and the notification system will streamline the process and enhance the overall user experience.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Fast-track disability claims, starting with PACT Act claims

**Explanation:** This priority directly impacts veterans who are actively using the VA claims status portal to track their claim progress by expediting the process for them.

**Additional Priorities:**
- **Improve the Veteran login experience:** Enhancing the login experience will make it easier for veterans to access the claims status portal and track their benefits.
- **Enhance Veterans' personalized online experience:** Customizing the online experience for veterans using the claims status portal will improve their overall satisfaction and engagement with the system.

---

## 🧭 User Journey

**User Journey Connection:** The VA Disability Benefits Claims Tracking and Status Portal serves as a crucial tool for Veterans to monitor the progress of their disability claims and stay informed about their benefits.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Taking care of myself:** Accessing the portal to check the status of their disability claim
- **Putting down roots:** Uploading necessary documents for their claim through the portal
- **Retiring:** Receiving notifications and updates on their claim status through the portal

**Critical Pain Points (if applicable):** Veterans may experience frustration if the portal is not user-friendly or if there are delays in updating their claim status.

---

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify specific pain points in the VA Disability Benefits Claims Tracking and Status Portal that cause confusion or anxiety for veterans during the claims process.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Understand the specific features or functionalities of the portal that veterans find most challenging or frustrating.
- Determine the frequency and impact of these pain points on veterans' overall experience with the claims process.
- Gather actionable insights to inform targeted improvements to the portal that address identified pain points.

---

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** "Interviews" are optimal for this research as they allow for in-depth exploration of veterans' experiences and pain points with the VA claims status portal in a controlled setting.

**Implementation Approach:**
- Conduct 1-hour semi-structured interviews with each participant to gather detailed insights
- Use task-based scenarios to observe participants' interactions with the portal and identify pain points
- Implement a post-interview survey to gather quantitative data on usability and satisfaction with the portal

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- Submit research plan for Collaboration Cycle approval at least 2 weeks prior to data collection
- Provide trauma-informed care training for researchers conducting interviews
- Offer remote or in-person accessibility accommodations as needed for participants

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges:** Difficulty in finding qualified Veterans who meet the specific criteria for participation
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Reach out to Veteran Service Organizations (VSOs) and VA hospitals to help identify and recruit eligible participants. Offer incentives such as gift cards or vouchers to increase participation.

**Execution issues:** Problems with the implementation of the ["interviews"] methodology
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Conduct pilot interviews with a small group of participants to identify and address any potential issues with the interview process. Provide training to interviewers to ensure consistency and quality in data collection.

**Timeline risks:** Delays in the research process that impact the standard timeline
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Develop a detailed project plan with clear milestones and deadlines. Regularly monitor progress and address any issues promptly to prevent delays. Allocate additional resources if necessary to stay on track.

**Quality concerns:** Risks related to data validity and stakeholder satisfaction
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Mitigation:** Implement rigorous data validation processes to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the collected data. Engage stakeholders throughout the research process to gather feedback and address any concerns or issues promptly.
