# 🧭 Research Plan – VA Electronic Health Record Workflow Optimization

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "VA Electronic Health Record (EHR) System and Clinical Workflow Tools - Digitally manage Veterans' health records and streamline medical processes. Essential for efficient care coordination within the VA healthcare system.

**🏛️ Team/Office:** Veterans Health Administration (VHA) Office of Health Informatics  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** Lapedra Tolson  
**🗓️ Date:** July 17, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

This research will inform the decision to redesign the VA Electronic Health Record (EHR) System and Clinical Workflow Tools to reduce documentation time and improve patient care efficiency for VA clinical staff. This change will directly benefit Veterans by streamlining the note templates and medication ordering system, ultimately enhancing the overall quality of care provided. The outcome will result in increased efficiency and effectiveness in delivering healthcare services to our nation's Veterans.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Improve the Veteran login experience

**Explanation:** Enhancing the login experience for Veterans accessing the VA Electronic Health Record (EHR) System and Clinical Workflow Tools will increase usability and efficiency for this audience.

- **[Life Stages]:** Accessing medical records and treatment history

**Critical Pain Points (if applicable):** Ensuring secure and seamless integration of EHR system with existing VA healthcare services.

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify specific workflow inefficiencies and usability barriers within the VA EHR system to enhance clinical staff efficiency and satisfaction during patient encounters and documentation tasks.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Pinpoint areas of the EHR system that cause delays or errors in patient documentation processes.
- Identify opportunities to streamline navigation and data entry within the EHR system for clinical staff.
- Evaluate the impact of identified usability issues on clinical staff productivity and overall patient care quality.

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** 
"Usability testing is optimal for this research as it allows for direct observation of user interactions with the EHR system, pinpointing specific workflow inefficiencies and usability barriers."

**Implementation Approach:**
- Conduct moderated usability testing sessions with participants using thinking-aloud protocol for real-time feedback.
- Utilize task-based scenarios to simulate common clinical workflows and observe user interactions with the EHR system.
- Implement accessibility accommodations for participants with diverse needs, ensuring a comprehensive evaluation of the system's usability. 

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- Submit Collaboration Cycle documentation for approval at least 4 weeks prior to research initiation.
- Incorporate trauma-informed research practices to create a safe and supportive environment for participants.
- Provide accessibility accommodations such as screen readers and adjustable font sizes for participants with disabilities.

**Execution issues:** Problems with implementing ["usability"] methodology
- **Likelihood:** Low

**Timeline risks:** Delays impacting the standard timeline
- **Likelihood:** High
