## 🔎 Background

This research will inform a decision about implementing a new mental health program for Veterans. By providing targeted support, Veterans will experience improved mental well-being and overall quality of life. The VA will see increased efficiency and effectiveness in mental health services, leading to better outcomes for Veterans and reduced costs for the organization.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

Primary OCTO Priority: Improve the Veteran login experience

Explanation: Hello aims to provide a seamless and user-friendly login experience for Veterans accessing VA.gov.

Additional Priorities:
- Solidify the VA.gov platform: Ensuring the stability and reliability of the platform is crucial for Hello to deliver its services effectively.
- Enhance Veterans' personalized online experience: Hello can tailor its services to meet the individual needs and preferences of Veterans, improving overall satisfaction and engagement.

---

## 🧭 User Journey

**User Journey Connection:** Hello serves as a vital tool for veterans to connect with healthcare providers, mental health resources, and fellow veterans throughout their journey.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Taking care of myself:** Hello provides access to telehealth services, mental health support, and resources for managing physical health, helping veterans prioritize their well-being.
- **Putting down roots:** Hello offers community connections, job opportunities, and housing assistance to help veterans establish stability and support in civilian life.
- **Retiring:** Hello assists veterans in navigating retirement benefits, financial planning, and social activities to ensure a smooth transition into this new phase of life.

**Critical Pain Points:**
- Limited access to mental health resources and support.
- Difficulty in finding employment and housing opportunities.
- Confusion surrounding retirement benefits and financial planning.

---

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Measure user satisfaction with the Hello app by conducting a survey to gather feedback on its usability and features.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Identify pain points in the Hello app through usability testing to improve user experience.
- Conduct interviews with Hello users to understand their specific needs and preferences for future updates.
- Analyze user data to determine the most popular features and prioritize them in decision-making for the Hello app.

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Usability research is specifically right for Hello because it allows us to observe how users interact with the platform in real-time, identify pain points, and gather feedback to improve user experience.

**Implementation Approach:**
- **Session Format:** One-on-one usability testing sessions lasting approximately 30-60 minutes.
- **Key Activities:** Task-based scenarios to observe how users navigate Hello, thinking aloud protocol to capture user thought process, and post-test interviews for feedback.
- **Evaluation Protocol:** Use a combination of quantitative metrics (task completion rates, time on task) and qualitative data (user feedback, observations) to evaluate the user experience of Hello.

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- **Collaboration Cycle:** Ensure collaboration with VA stakeholders throughout the research process, including regular check-ins and updates on progress.
- **Trauma-Informed Approach:** Sensitivity to potential trauma triggers for veteran participants, provide a safe and supportive environment for participation.
- **Accessibility:** Provide accommodations such as screen readers, large font options, and alternative input methods for participants with diverse needs.
