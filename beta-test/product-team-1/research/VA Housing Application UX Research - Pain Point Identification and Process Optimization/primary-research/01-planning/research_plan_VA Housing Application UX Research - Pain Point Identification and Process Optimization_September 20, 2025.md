# 🧭 Research Plan – VA Housing Application UX Research - Pain Point Identification and Process Optimization

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "VA.gov Housing Benefits Application Portal - facilitates online application, eligibility screening, document upload, and application status tracking for Veterans seeking housing assistance. This tool streamlines the process, providing Veterans with a user-friendly platform to access crucial benefits efficiently within the VA ecosystem."

**🏛️ Team/Office:** Primary Team: VA Digital Experience Team
Collaborating Office: Office of Veterans Experience (VEO)
Support: VA Housing Benefits Administration
Technical Partner: VA.gov Platform Team
Accessibility Consultant: Section 508 Compliance Office  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** Jordan Smith  
**🗓️ Date:** September 20, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

This research will inform decisions on redesigning the VA.gov Housing Benefits Application Portal, focusing on the online application workflow, eligibility screening, document upload system, and application status tracking. The changes will simplify the application process for Veterans, reducing support calls and improving completion rates. This will enhance user experience and streamline access to housing benefits for those who have served our country.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Fast-track disability claims, starting with PACT Act claims

**Explanation:** This priority directly aligns with the goal of improving the online application workflow for VA housing benefits by streamlining the disability claims process for veterans.

**Additional Priorities (if applicable):**
- **Solidify the VA.gov platform:** Ensure the stability and reliability of the online portal for veterans applying for housing benefits.
- **Enhance Veterans' personalized online experience:** Tailor the user experience of the application portal to meet the unique needs of individual veterans. 

**Project-Specific Priorities:**
- Improve eligibility screening process for veterans applying for housing benefits
- Enhance document upload system for easier submission of required paperwork
- Implement real-time application status tracking for veterans to monitor progress of their housing benefits application.

---

## 🧭 User Journey

**User Journey Connection:** The VA.gov Housing Benefits Application Portal is a crucial tool for Veterans to apply for housing benefits, screen their eligibility, upload necessary documents, and track their application status seamlessly within their journey of Putting down roots or Retiring.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Putting down roots:** Completing the online application form for housing benefits
- **Retiring:** Uploading required documents for eligibility screening
- **Retiring:** Tracking the status of their housing benefits application

**Critical Pain Points (if applicable):** Veterans may face challenges in navigating the document upload system or understanding the eligibility criteria, leading to potential delays in their application process.

---

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify and prioritize the top 3-5 usability barriers contributing to the 34% application abandonment rate for veterans applying for housing benefits online, uncovering actionable insights to address these issues effectively.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Uncover common pain points in the online application workflow that hinder completion for veterans
- Identify key areas of confusion or frustration in the eligibility screening process that deter users from proceeding
- Evaluate the effectiveness of the document upload system and application status tracking features in meeting veterans' needs and expectations

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Usability testing, interviews, and competitive analysis are chosen for their ability to directly uncover usability barriers and root causes behind high application abandonment rates.

**Implementation Approach:**
- One-hour user interviews with 5 participants to gather in-depth insights
- 45-minute usability testing sessions with 5 participants to assess task completion and user experience
- Detailed evaluation of VA.gov Housing Benefits Application Portal components, focusing on online application workflow, eligibility screening, document upload system, and application status tracking

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- Ensure Collaboration Cycle requirements are met within specified approval timeline
- Incorporate trauma-informed research considerations and provide necessary accessibility accommodations for participants.

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges:** Difficulty in finding qualified participants who meet the specific criteria
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Partner with veteran organizations and VA facilities to reach out to potential participants directly, offer incentives for participation, and expand recruitment efforts to online veteran communities and social media platforms.

**Execution issues:** Problems with implementing the usability testing, interviews, and competitive analysis effectively
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Conduct thorough training sessions for the research team on the methodology, ensure clear communication and coordination between team members, and conduct pilot testing to identify and address any potential issues before the official sessions begin.

**Timeline risks:** Delays in the research process that may impact the standard timeline
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Mitigation:** Develop a detailed project plan with clear milestones and deadlines, regularly monitor progress against the plan, and proactively address any obstacles or delays that arise to ensure the project stays on track.

**Quality concerns:** Potential issues with data validity and stakeholder satisfaction
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Implement rigorous data validation processes, regularly review and analyze the collected data to ensure accuracy, and involve stakeholders throughout the research process to gather feedback and address any concerns promptly.
