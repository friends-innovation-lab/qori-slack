## 🧪 Task Sections

## 🎯 Task 1: Understanding Current Usage Patterns – 10 Minutes

**Instructions for Moderator:**
1. Ask participants to describe how they currently use the product or service.
2. Prompt them to explain any specific features or functionalities they frequently use.
3. Encourage participants to share any challenges or pain points they encounter during usage.

**Things to Watch For:**
- Participants' ability to articulate their current usage patterns clearly.
- Identification of common pain points or challenges faced during usage.
- Engagement level and enthusiasm when discussing current usage.

## 🎯 Task 2: Exploring Desired Features and Functionalities – 15 Minutes

**Instructions for Moderator:**
1. Present participants with a list of potential features and functionalities for the product or service.
2. Ask them to rank these features in order of importance to them.
3. Encourage participants to explain why they prioritize certain features over others.

**Things to Watch For:**
- Participants' ability to prioritize features based on their needs and preferences.
- Depth of reasoning provided for ranking certain features higher than others.
- Consistency in feature preferences among participants.

## 🎯 Task 3: Evaluating User Interface and User Experience – 10 Minutes

**Instructions for Moderator:**
1. Show participants screenshots or prototypes of the product's user interface.
2. Ask them to provide feedback on the design, layout, and overall user experience.
3. Encourage participants to suggest any improvements or changes they would like to see.

**Things to Watch For:**
- Participants' initial reactions to the user interface design.
- Specific feedback on what aspects of the user experience are working well or need improvement.
- Creativity and innovation in participants' suggestions for enhancing the user interface.

## 🎤 Post-Task Interview – 10 Minutes
- How did you feel about the overall structure and organization of the tasks in relation to the research objectives?
- Were there any specific areas or aspects of the tasks that you found challenging or confusing?
- Can you provide any feedback on how well the tasks aligned with the research goals and objectives?
- In hindsight, do you feel that the tasks effectively captured the information needed to address the research objectives?
- How do you think the tasks could be improved or modified to better align with the research goals in the future?
