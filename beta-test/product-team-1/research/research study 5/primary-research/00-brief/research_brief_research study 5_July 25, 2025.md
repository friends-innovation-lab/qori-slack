Study 5 aims to investigate the impact of social media usage on mental health outcomes among adolescents. By examining the relationship between social media usage and mental health, this study aims to provide valuable insights for developing strategies to promote positive mental health in adolescents.

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans encounter barriers in accessing mental health resources within the VA system.
- Which barriers veterans prioritize as the most significant in hindering their access to mental health services.
- Why veterans perceive certain barriers as more challenging than others in seeking mental health support.
- How veterans currently navigate the VA system to access mental health resources and services.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

**Primary questions:**
1. How do veterans currently navigate the process of accessing mental health resources within the VA system, considering the barriers identified in research study 5?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from seeking mental health support through non-VA resources, as outlined in the user flows from research study 5?
3. Why do veterans experience delays in receiving mental health care within the VA system, as indicated in the research approach of study 5?
4. What causes veterans to feel disconnected or unsupported in their mental health journey, based on the user flows and barriers highlighted in research study 5?
