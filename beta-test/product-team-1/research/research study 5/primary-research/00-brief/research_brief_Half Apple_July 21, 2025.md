## 🎯 Business Context

In this study, we aim to investigate the impact of consuming half an apple daily on overall health and well-being. The research will focus on examining the potential health benefits associated with incorporating this simple dietary habit into one's daily routine. By exploring the effects of consuming half an apple, we hope to provide valuable insights into the role of fruits in promoting a healthy lifestyle.

**Stakeholder Concerns:**
- Inefficient communication between departments is causing delays in project timelines and impacting overall productivity.
- Users are experiencing frustration due to outdated technology systems that are hindering their ability to perform their job effectively.
- Stakeholders want to address the lack of data security measures in place, which is putting sensitive information at risk of being compromised.

---

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans encounter specific pain points in the user flow when trying to access resources and support services.
- Which priorities are most critical for veterans when navigating the user flow for accessing resources and support services.
- Why veterans are experiencing certain root causes of frustration and confusion within the user flow for accessing resources and support services.
- How veterans currently navigate the user flow for accessing resources and support services and where improvements can be made.

**User journey in scope:**
1. Open the Half Apple app on your device.
2. Sign in using your email and password or create a new account if you're a new user.
3. Browse through the different categories or use the search bar to find a specific item.
4. Select the item you want to purchase and add it to your cart.
5. Review your cart to make sure you have everything you need.
6. Proceed to checkout and enter your shipping and payment information.
7. Confirm your order and wait for a confirmation email.
8. Track your order using the tracking number provided in the email.
9. Once your order arrives, enjoy your Half Apple products!

## 🎯 Target Barriers

- Users are confused by the ambiguous name "Half Apple" and do not understand the purpose or function of the product
- Potential customers are hesitant to purchase due to unclear product descriptions and lack of detailed information on features and benefits
- The branding and marketing materials do not effectively communicate the unique selling points of "Half Apple" and fail to differentiate it from competitors

---

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

**Primary questions:**
1. How do veterans currently navigate the process of accessing healthcare services through Half Apple's platform?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from efficiently utilizing Half Apple's platform to schedule appointments and communicate with healthcare providers?
3. Why do veterans experience difficulties in understanding the user flows within Half Apple's platform for managing their healthcare needs?
4. What causes veterans to encounter challenges in accessing necessary information and resources on Half Apple's platform for their healthcare needs?

**Team hypotheses:**
- Limited access to affordable transportation increases missed medical appointments among low-income patients.
- Lack of communication between healthcare providers and caregivers leads to increased medication errors among elderly patients.
- Inadequate mental health resources in schools contribute to higher rates of student absenteeism and academic underachievement.

## 🧭 Proposed Research Approach

**High-level approach:** Conduct user interviews and surveys to gather insights on consumer preferences and behaviors related to apple consumption. Analyze the data to identify trends and patterns that can inform product development and marketing strategies.

**Reasoning:** This refined research approach makes sense for the research objectives because it allows for in-depth exploration of consumer behavior and preferences through qualitative methods, providing insights into the underlying motivations and attitudes driving their decisions. This approach aligns with the scope of the research objectives by offering a more nuanced understanding of the target audience within the constraints of the available resources.
