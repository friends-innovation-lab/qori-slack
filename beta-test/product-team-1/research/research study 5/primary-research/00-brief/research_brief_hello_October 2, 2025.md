## 🎯 Business Context

Greetings,

This study aims to investigate the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and veterans in order to better understand their specific needs and improve the support services available to them. By examining the experiences and obstacles encountered by this population, we hope to identify areas for intervention and enhancement within the VA system. This research is crucial in addressing the complex issues that impact the well-being and quality of life of our nation's veterans.

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans encounter barriers in the hello platform that hinder their user experience.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. How do veterans currently navigate the hello platform in terms of user flows and interactions?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from effectively utilizing the features and resources provided by hello?
3. Why do veterans encounter difficulties in accessing and utilizing hello's services despite the availability of user flows?
4. What causes veterans to disengage or become frustrated with hello's interface and functionality, leading to potential drop-off in usage?

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
- The lack of a user-friendly interface in hello increases user frustration and decreases engagement with the platform.
- Veterans with PTSD face more difficulties in navigating the hello app due to its complex layout and lack of clear instructions.
- The current design of hello, which requires multiple steps to access important features, leads to decreased usage and overall satisfaction among users.
