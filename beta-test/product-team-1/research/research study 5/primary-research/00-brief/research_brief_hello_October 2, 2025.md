## 🎯 Business Context

Greetings, 

This study aims to explore the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and Veterans in order to better understand and address their specific needs. By delving into the complexities of VA and Veteran-specific issues, we hope to contribute valuable insights that can inform and improve support services for this population.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Difficulty in accessing and navigating the "hello" feature is causing frustration for Veterans trying to connect with VA services remotely
- Inadequate training and support for VA staff on how to effectively use the "hello" feature is impacting the efficiency and quality of service delivery
- Technical glitches and compatibility issues with different devices are hindering the seamless integration of the "hello" feature into the VA system and workflow

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans encounter barriers in using hello.
- Which steps in the user flow are causing the most frustration for veterans.
- Why certain study contexts may impact the effectiveness of hello for veterans.
- How veterans currently navigate through hello and what challenges they face.

## 🎯 Target Barriers

- Users are unable to locate the "hello" button due to poor visual hierarchy and lack of clear labeling
- Users experience confusion when trying to initiate a conversation as the "hello" feature is buried within multiple layers of navigation
- Users are discouraged from using the "hello" function due to slow loading times and unresponsive interface elements

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. "How do veterans currently navigate the process of accessing mental health resources through hello?"
2. "What barriers prevent veterans from effectively utilizing the user flows within hello to connect with mental health support services?"
3. "Why do veterans encounter difficulties in following the prescribed user flows within hello for accessing mental health resources?"
4. "What causes veterans to experience frustration or confusion when attempting to utilize hello for mental health support?"

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
1. The lack of user-friendly interface in hello increases user frustration and decreases overall satisfaction with the service.
2. Veterans with PTSD face more difficulty in navigating the hello platform, leading to lower engagement and utilization rates.
3. The current design of hello's notification system leads to users missing important updates and information, resulting in decreased user engagement.
