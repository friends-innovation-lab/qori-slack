## 🎯 Business Context

Greetings, 

This study aims to investigate the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and veterans in accessing healthcare services. By exploring the specific barriers and limitations encountered by this demographic, we seek to identify areas for improvement and enhance the overall quality of care provided to our nation's veterans.

## 🎯 Target Barriers

- Users are confused by the lack of clear call-to-action buttons on the homepage
- Users struggle to navigate the menu due to inconsistent labeling and organization
- Users encounter errors when attempting to submit forms due to lack of validation messages
- Users find it difficult to locate important information due to poor contrast and font choices
- Users experience slow loading times on certain pages, leading to frustration and abandonment

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. How do veterans currently navigate the process of accessing mental health services through the hello platform?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from effectively utilizing the user flows within the hello platform to connect with mental health resources?
3. Why do veterans experience difficulties in finding and utilizing the specific features within the hello platform designed to support their mental health needs?
4. What causes veterans to disengage or feel frustrated with the user experience of the hello platform when seeking mental health support?

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
1. The lack of clear instructions in hello increases user confusion and leads to a decrease in successful interactions.
2. Veterans with PTSD face more difficulty in navigating the features of hello, resulting in a higher rate of user frustration.
3. The current design of hello's user interface leads to longer response times and a decrease in overall user satisfaction.
