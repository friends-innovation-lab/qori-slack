## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans are experiencing the most frustration in the current user flows.
- Which barriers are causing the most significant disruptions in the user experience for veterans.
- Why certain barriers are consistently leading to errors or confusion for veterans navigating the system.
- How veterans are currently adapting to overcome obstacles within the user flows.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

**Primary questions:**
1. How do veterans currently navigate the process of accessing mental health resources within the VA system, as outlined in user flows from research study 5?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from effectively utilizing mental health services within the VA system, as identified in research study 5?
3. Why do veterans experience delays or difficulties in receiving timely mental health care within the VA system, as highlighted in research study 5?
4. What causes veterans to encounter challenges in communicating their mental health needs and concerns to healthcare providers within the VA system, as indicated in research study 5?

**Team hypotheses:**
1. Lack of access to affordable transportation increases missed medical appointments among low-income patients.
2. Limited knowledge of available resources leads to underutilization of mental health services among college students.
3. Inadequate communication between healthcare providers results in medication errors among elderly patients with multiple chronic conditions.
