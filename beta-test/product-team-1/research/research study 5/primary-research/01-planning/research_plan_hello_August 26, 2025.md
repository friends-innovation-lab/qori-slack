# 🧭 Research Plan – hello

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "hello - a user-friendly platform for Veterans to access personalized health information and resources. Improves efficiency in VA healthcare delivery and empowers Veterans to take control of their well-being."

**🏛️ Team/Office:** hello  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** hello  
**🗓️ Date:** August 26, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

This research will inform the decision to redesign the hello feature for easier navigation. This change will improve Veterans' ability to access important information quickly and efficiently. Ultimately, this enhancement will lead to increased user satisfaction and reduced frustration when using the hello product area.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Improve the Veteran login experience

**Explanation:** By focusing on improving the Veteran login experience, we can ensure that veterans have easy access to the resources and services they need on the VA.gov platform.

**Additional Priorities:**
- **Solidify the VA.gov platform:** Ensuring a stable and reliable platform is essential for providing a seamless user experience for veterans.
- **Fast-track disability claims, starting with PACT Act claims:** Streamlining the disability claims process will help veterans receive the support they need in a timely manner.

---

## 🧭 User Journey

**Detected Audience:** Veterans

**User Journey Connection:** hello is integrated into the VA Journey of Veterans Map to assist veterans in their life stages, providing support and resources for various needs.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Taking care of myself:** Accessing health and wellness resources through hello
- **Putting down roots:** Finding housing assistance and community support via hello
- **Retiring:** Exploring retirement benefits and financial planning tools on hello

**Critical Pain Points (if applicable):** Ensuring user-friendly interface for easy navigation and accessibility for veterans with varying levels of tech proficiency.

---

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify key pain points and usability issues in the hello feature through user interviews to inform design improvements.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Understand user behaviors and motivations when using the hello feature.
- Gather feedback on specific features of hello to prioritize enhancements.
- Identify opportunities for hello to better meet user needs and expectations.

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Interviews are chosen for their ability to gather in-depth insights and perspectives directly from participants, allowing for a more personalized understanding of their experiences.

**Implementation Approach:**
- Conduct 1-hour semi-structured interviews with each participant
- Utilize open-ended questions and probes to elicit detailed responses
- Implement a debriefing session to validate findings and gather additional insights

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- Submit Collaboration Cycle request by [date] for approval
- Incorporate trauma-informed language and practices throughout the research process
- Provide accessibility accommodations such as closed captioning and alternative formats for participants with disabilities.

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges: Difficulty finding qualified participants**
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Reach out to specific VA departments directly to identify potential participants, offer incentives for participation, and utilize social media and online platforms to expand recruitment efforts.

**Execution issues: Problems with interview implementation**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Conduct pilot interviews with colleagues to identify and address any potential issues with the interview process, provide detailed instructions and training for interviewers, and have a backup plan in place in case of technical difficulties.

**Timeline risks: Delays impacting rush timeline**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Develop a detailed project timeline with specific milestones and deadlines, regularly communicate progress updates to stakeholders, prioritize tasks to ensure critical steps are completed on time, and be prepared to adjust the timeline as needed.

**Quality concerns: Data validity and stakeholder satisfaction**
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Use standardized interview protocols to ensure consistency, conduct thorough data analysis to verify findings, involve stakeholders in the research process to gather feedback and ensure alignment with expectations, and address any concerns or discrepancies promptly.
