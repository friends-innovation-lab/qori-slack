## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** The combination of ["usability","interviews"] is optimal for research study 5 as it allows for both quantitative data on usability metrics and qualitative insights from participant interviews, providing a comprehensive understanding of the product area.

**Implementation Approach:**
- The sessions will be conducted in a one-on-one format with each participant, lasting approximately 60 minutes.
- Key activities will include task-based usability testing to assess the product's functionality and user experience, followed by semi-structured interviews to gather in-depth feedback.
- Data collection techniques will involve recording participant interactions with the product, taking notes during the interviews, and analyzing both quantitative and qualitative data to identify patterns and insights.
- Specific protocols for research study 5 evaluation will include a pre-defined set of tasks for participants to complete, a standardized interview guide with open-ended questions, and a structured approach to analyzing and synthesizing the data.

**VA Compliance Notes:**
- IRB requirements have been submitted, and approval is expected within the next two weeks.
- Trauma-informed research considerations will be taken into account by ensuring a safe and supportive environment for participants, providing clear information about the study, and offering resources for any emotional distress.
- Accessibility accommodations for participants will be made, such as providing alternative formats for materials, ensuring physical accessibility to the research space, and offering support for participants with specific needs.
