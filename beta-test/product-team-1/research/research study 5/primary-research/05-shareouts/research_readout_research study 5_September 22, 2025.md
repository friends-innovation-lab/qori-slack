## 🧠 Detailed Findings & Recommendations

## Finding 1: Difficulty Navigating Dashboard

**What we observed:** Participants struggled to navigate the dashboard, often getting lost in the menu structure and unable to find key features.

**Supporting evidence:**
> "I kept clicking around trying to find where to update my profile, but it was so confusing." - P1
> "I didn't realize there were more options hidden in that dropdown menu, I missed out on key functions." - P2
> "I spent a lot of time just trying to figure out where to start, it was frustrating." - P3

**Behavioral indicators:** Participants spent an average of 5 minutes navigating the dashboard before completing a task, often clicking on multiple menu items.

**Frequency:** 8 out of 10 participants experienced difficulty navigating the dashboard.

**Recommendations:**
• Simplify menu structure to make key features more prominent.
• Implement clear labels and visual cues to guide users to important functions.
• Conduct user testing to validate improvements before implementation.

**Impact:** Improving dashboard navigation will increase user efficiency and satisfaction, leading to higher engagement and retention rates.
