## 🔍 Key Findings

### Finding 1: Login Process Complexity

**What we observed:**
The participant struggled with the complex login process, leading to frustration.

**Evidence:**
> "I couldn't figure out which password to use, it was really confusing." — PT002

**Frequency:** 1 of 2 participants
**Severity:** Critical

**Recommendation:**
Provide clear instructions and guidance for the login process to reduce user confusion.

---

### Finding 2: Missing Search Functionality

**What we observed:**
The absence of a search function hindered user efficiency.

**Evidence:**
> "I wish there was a search bar, it would have saved me a lot of time." — PT002

**Frequency:** 1 of 2 participants
**Severity:** High

**Recommendation:**
Implement a search bar feature to enhance user efficiency and satisfaction.

---

### Finding 3: Unclear Navigation Labels

**What we observed:**
Confusion arose from unclear navigation labels.

**Evidence:**
> "I wasn't sure where to find the settings, the labels were not very clear." — PT002

**Frequency:** 1 of 2 participants
**Severity:** Medium

**Recommendation:**
Improve navigation labels to make it easier for users to locate specific sections within the interface.
