## Interview Guide

### Opening (3-5 min)

> *Build rapport and set context*

- Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching mobile app experience and wanted to understand the technical landscape better.
- Everything you share helps us design better solutions. No wrong answers.
- Okay to record? [If applicable]

**Opener:** "To start, can you tell me about your role and how you interact with the mobile development process?"

---

### Section: Technical Constraints (15 min)

> *Understand technical limitations affecting mobile app development and performance*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What are the biggest technical constraints your team faces when building mobile features? | • How do these constraints impact development timelines? • Which constraint causes the most user-facing issues? |
| 2 | Walk me through the mobile app's architecture and where you see bottlenecks. | • What parts of the system are most fragile? • Where do performance issues typically originate? |
| 3 | How do third-party integrations or legacy systems affect what you can build? | • Which dependencies cause the most problems? • What workarounds has your team had to implement? |
| 4 | What technical debt is currently impacting the mobile team's velocity? | • How do you prioritize technical debt against new features? • What would you fix first if you had unlimited resources? |

**Listen for:** Specific system limitations, performance bottlenecks, integration challenges, resource allocation conflicts

---

### Section: Backstage Operations (15 min)

> *Understand internal processes and operations that affect mobile app functionality*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | Walk me through how a mobile feature goes from concept to production. | • Where do delays typically happen? • Which handoffs are most problematic? |
| 2 | How does your team handle mobile app releases and deployments? | • What's your rollback process if something breaks? • How do you coordinate with other teams during releases? |
| 3 | What happens when users report issues with the mobile app? | • How do you prioritize and triage mobile-specific bugs? • What visibility do you have into production issues? |
| 4 | How do you monitor mobile app performance and user behavior? | • What metrics matter most to your team? • How do backend issues surface in mobile monitoring? |
| 5 | Describe how your team collaborates with design, product, and other engineering teams. | • Where do communication breakdowns typically occur? • What processes work well versus poorly? |

**Listen for:** Process inefficiencies, communication gaps, monitoring blind spots, cross-team dependencies

---

### Section: Resource Constraints (10 min)

> *Understand how team capacity and resources impact mobile development*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | How does your team's current capacity affect what you can deliver? | • What would you prioritize differently with more resources? • Which skills are you missing on the team? |
| 2 | How do you balance maintaining existing mobile features versus building new ones? | • What percentage of time goes to maintenance versus new development? • How do you make these trade-off decisions? |
| 3 | What external dependencies or approvals slow down mobile development? | • Which stakeholders need to sign off on mobile changes? • How do security or compliance requirements impact your work? |

**Listen for:** Team capacity issues, skill gaps, competing priorities, external blockers

---

### Closing (3-5 min)

- "What haven't I asked about that you think I should know?"
- "Who else should I talk to about mobile development processes?"
- "Can I follow up if I have more questions?"

**Thank them for their time.**
