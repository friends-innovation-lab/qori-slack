## **Opportunities**

**Persona: Sarah, the Busy Parent**

💡 **Primary Opportunity:** Streamline the checkout process to save time for busy parents.

🛠️ **Implementation Approach:**
- Simplify the checkout flow by reducing the number of steps required.
- Implement saved payment methods for quick and easy transactions.
- Add an option for express checkout for returning customers.

📈 **Success Metrics:** Decrease in cart abandonment rate, increase in completed transactions, and positive feedback on checkout process.

⚡ **Quick Wins:** 
- Add a "Buy Now" button for instant purchases.
- Offer one-click checkout for repeat purchases.

🎯 **Relevant Use Case:** Sarah's need for a quick and efficient checkout process can inform the design of a mobile app feature that allows for instant purchases with minimal steps.

⭐ **Priority Level:** High - Sarah's frustration with the current checkout process directly impacts her ability to make purchases efficiently.
