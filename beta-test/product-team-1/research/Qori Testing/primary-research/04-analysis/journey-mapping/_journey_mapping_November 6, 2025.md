## **Opportunities**

**Stage: "Awareness"**

💡 **Primary Opportunity:** Develop targeted marketing campaigns to reach potential users who may benefit from Qori Testing.
💡 **Secondary Opportunity:** Utilize social media influencers or partnerships to increase brand awareness.
🛠️ **Implementation Approach:** Collaborate with marketing team to identify key target audiences and create tailored messaging.
📈 **Success Metrics:** Measure increase in website traffic, social media engagement, and new user sign-ups.
⭐ **Priority Level:** High - Increasing awareness is crucial for attracting new users.

**Stage: "Research"**

💡 **Primary Opportunity:** Provide easily accessible information on the benefits of Qori Testing and how it differs from competitors.
💡 **Secondary Opportunity:** Offer a free trial or demo for users to experience the product before committing.
🛠️ **Implementation Approach:** Update website with clear product descriptions, case studies, and comparison charts.
📈 **Success Metrics:** Track conversion rates from website visits to sign-ups for free trials.
⭐ **Priority Level:** Medium - Improving research stage can lead to higher conversion rates.

**Stage: "Decision Making"**

💡 **Primary Opportunity:** Simplify pricing structure and clearly outline the value proposition of Qori Testing.
💡 **Secondary Opportunity:** Implement a live chat feature for users to ask questions and receive real-time support.
🛠️ **Implementation Approach:** Conduct user testing to identify pain points in decision-making process and iterate on pricing page design.
📈 **Success Metrics:** Measure increase in completed purchases and decrease in abandoned carts.
⭐ **Priority Level:** High - Decision-making stage directly impacts revenue generation.

**Stage: "Post-Purchase Experience"**

💡 **Primary Opportunity:** Develop onboarding materials and tutorials to help new users get started with Qori Testing.
💡 **Secondary Opportunity:** Implement a feedback system to gather insights on user experience and areas for improvement.
🛠️ **Implementation Approach:** Create a series of onboarding emails, videos, and guides to support new users.
📈 **Success Metrics:** Monitor user engagement with onboarding materials and track customer satisfaction scores.
⭐ **Priority Level:** Medium - Enhancing post-purchase experience can lead to higher user retention rates.
