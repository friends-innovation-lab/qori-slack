## Theme 1: Multiple Entry Points Create Confusion

**Frequency:** 32 of 76 responses (42%)

**Sentiment:** 😤 Negative

**What respondents said:**

> "I got a text, an email, and something in MyHealtheVet. They all had different links. I didn't know which one to click." — R001

**Pattern:** Participants expressed frustration and confusion due to receiving appointment information through various channels, leading to difficulty in accessing the correct link.

## Theme 2: Fear of Irreversible Actions

**Frequency:** 24 of 76 responses (32%)

**Sentiment:** 😤 Negative
