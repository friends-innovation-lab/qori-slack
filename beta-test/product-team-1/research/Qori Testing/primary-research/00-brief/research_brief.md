## 🎯 Business Context

The study aims to investigate the challenges specific to Veterans Affairs (VA) and veterans in conducting research. By examining the unique obstacles faced by VA and veterans, this research seeks to identify potential solutions to improve the testing process in these populations. Understanding the distinct challenges in testing research within the VA and veteran community is crucial for enhancing the quality and effectiveness of research outcomes in this demographic.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Difficulty in accessing timely and accurate test results is causing frustration and delays in treatment for Veterans
- Inaccurate or unreliable testing methods are leading to misdiagnoses and ineffective treatment plans for Veterans
- Lack of user-friendly interfaces or clear instructions on how to use testing equipment is hindering Veterans' ability to self-administer tests accurately and efficiently

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans encounter obstacles or confusion in the user flow of the Qori Testing platform.
- Which features or functionalities are considered most crucial by veterans using the Qori Testing platform.
- Why veterans may be experiencing frustration or inefficiencies in their interactions with the Qori Testing platform.
- How veterans are currently navigating through the Qori Testing platform and where improvements can be made to enhance their experience.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. "How do veterans currently navigate the testing process for Qori Testing, from initial sign-up to receiving their results?"
2. "What barriers prevent veterans from completing the testing research tasks within the user flows provided by Qori Testing?"
3. "Why do veterans struggle with certain steps in the testing research process, as outlined in the user flows for Qori Testing?"
4. "What causes veterans to experience delays or confusion when trying to access and understand the results of their testing research with Qori Testing?"

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
- "The lack of user-friendly interface in Qori Testing increases the likelihood of user errors during testing sessions."
- "Veterans with PTSD face more difficulty in engaging with Qori Testing due to its triggering content."
- "The current design of Qori Testing leads to decreased user satisfaction and engagement levels."
