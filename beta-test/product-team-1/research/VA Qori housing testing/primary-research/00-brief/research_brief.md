## 🎯 Business Context

The study aims to investigate the housing conditions and challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) Qori residents. This research is crucial in understanding the unique housing needs of veterans and identifying potential areas for improvement in VA housing services. By examining the specific issues experienced by VA Qori residents, we can work towards enhancing the quality of housing provided to our nation's veterans.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Lack of accessibility features in VA Qori housing is causing difficulty for disabled Veterans to navigate and use the facilities, impacting their overall quality of life.
- Inefficient layout and design of VA Qori housing units is leading to longer wait times for Veterans to receive housing placements, affecting the timely delivery of services.
- Technical malfunctions in the smart home technology integrated into VA Qori housing units are preventing Veterans from utilizing key features, hindering their ability to live independently.

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- "Where veterans encounter the most challenges in navigating the VA Qori housing testing process."
- "Which aspects of the user flow are most critical for veterans seeking housing assistance through VA Qori."
- "Why veterans may experience delays or confusion in accessing VA Qori housing resources."
- "How veterans currently interact with the VA Qori housing testing platform and what pain points they encounter during the process."

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. How do veterans currently navigate the process of applying for VA Qori housing testing?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from accessing and utilizing VA Qori housing testing services effectively?
3. Why do veterans face challenges in understanding the user flows of VA Qori housing testing?
4. What causes veterans to experience difficulties in following the research approach outlined for VA Qori housing testing?

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
1. Lack of accessibility features in VA Qori housing testing increases the difficulty for veterans with physical disabilities to navigate and utilize the housing, resulting in decreased satisfaction and usage.
2. Veterans with PTSD face more challenges in adapting to the communal living environment of VA Qori housing testing, leading to increased stress and potential conflicts with other residents.
3. The current design of VA Qori housing testing, which focuses primarily on physical amenities, neglects the importance of mental health support services, resulting in a lack of resources for veterans struggling with psychological issues.
