## 🎯 Business Context

Greetings, the study aims to investigate the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and veterans in order to better understand their specific needs and improve support services. By focusing on VA and veteran-specific challenges, this research seeks to address gaps in current knowledge and enhance the quality of care provided to this population.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Difficulty navigating the hello system is causing frustration for Veterans trying to access important resources and information
- Inefficient hello processes are impacting VA operations by slowing down service delivery and increasing workload for staff
- Technical glitches in the hello platform are hindering Veterans from completing necessary tasks and obtaining necessary support

## 🎯 Target Barriers

- Users are unable to locate the "hello" button on the homepage due to poor visual hierarchy and lack of prominent placement
- Users experience confusion when the "hello" button is not labeled clearly, leading to uncertainty about its function
- Users encounter difficulty in accessing the "hello" feature on mobile devices due to unresponsive design or hidden navigation menus

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. How do veterans currently navigate the process of accessing mental health resources through hello?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from effectively utilizing the user flows within hello to connect with mental health support services?
3. Why do veterans experience difficulties in following the recommended user flows within hello for accessing mental health resources?
4. What causes veterans to encounter challenges in fully engaging with the research approach utilized by hello for improving mental health outcomes?

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
- The barrier of unfamiliarity with the app interface increases the likelihood of users abandoning the hello platform.
- Veterans with PTSD face more difficulty in navigating the app's mental health resources effectively.
- The current design of hello's notification system leads to users feeling overwhelmed and disengaged from utilizing the app regularly.
