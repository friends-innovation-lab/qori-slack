## 🎯 Business Context

The study aims to investigate the efficacy of various testing methods in assessing student comprehension and retention of course material. By analyzing the impact of different testing strategies on academic performance, this research seeks to provide insights into optimizing assessment practices in educational settings. This study is motivated by the need to enhance the validity and reliability of testing procedures to accurately measure student learning outcomes.

**Stakeholder Concerns:**
- Limited access to reliable transportation options is causing a decrease in employee productivity and attendance
- High cost of transportation is preventing low-income individuals from accessing essential services and opportunities
- Inadequate public transportation routes are hindering economic growth and development in certain areas

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans encounter the most significant barriers in the refined context of accessing mental health resources.
- Which barriers in the user flows for testing are the highest priorities for addressing based on user feedback.
- Why veterans experience specific pain points in the refined study context when trying to access mental health resources.
- How veterans currently navigate the user flows for testing and where improvements can be made to streamline the process.

**User journey in scope:**
1. Log in to the testing platform with your username and password.
2. Select the project you want to test from the dashboard.
3. Click on the "Test Cases" tab to view all available test cases for the project.
4. Choose a test case to execute by clicking on it.
5. Follow the instructions provided in the test case to perform the necessary actions.
6. Record the results of the test case, noting any issues or bugs encountered.
7. Mark the test case as "Pass" or "Fail" based on the outcome.
8. Provide detailed feedback on the test case, including any suggestions for improvement.
9. Repeat steps 4-8 for each test case assigned to you.
10. Once all test cases are completed, submit your test results for review by the project manager.

- Lack of standardized testing processes and methodologies

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

**Primary questions:**
1. How do veterans currently navigate the process of accessing mental health resources within the VA system?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from effectively following through with recommended mental health treatments and interventions?
3. Why do veterans struggle to engage with mental health support services provided by the VA?
4. What causes veterans to experience delays or obstacles in receiving timely mental health care through the VA system?

**Team hypotheses:**
1. Limited access to mental health services in rural areas leads to higher rates of untreated mental health conditions among farmers.
2. Lack of communication between healthcare providers and caregivers results in decreased quality of care for elderly patients.
3. Inadequate training for teachers on handling students with behavioral disorders leads to higher rates of disciplinary actions and lower academic performance among those students.
