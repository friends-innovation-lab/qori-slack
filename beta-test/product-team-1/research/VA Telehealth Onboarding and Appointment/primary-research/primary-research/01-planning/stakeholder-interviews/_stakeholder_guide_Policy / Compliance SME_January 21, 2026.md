## Interview Guide

### Opening (3-5 min)

> *Build rapport and set context*

- Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching VA Telehealth Onboarding and Appointments and wanted to understand the policy and compliance aspects better.
- Everything you share helps us design better solutions. No wrong answers.
- Okay to record? [If applicable]

**Opener:** "To start, can you tell me about your role and how you interact with the VA Telehealth system and policies?"

### Section 1: Constraints (Technical, Policy, Resource) (X min)

> *Understanding the constraints affecting the system*

| # | Question | Follow-ups |
|---|----------|------------|
| 1 | What are the biggest technical constraints affecting the performance of the VA Telehealth system? | • Can you provide examples of how these technical constraints impact user experience? |
| 2 | How do policy requirements influence the design and functionality of the system? | • Are there specific policy constraints that have posed challenges in the past? |

**Listen for:** Insights on how technical, policy, and resource constraints shape system operations.
