# 📁 README: Why Use an `artifacts/` Folder?

## 🎯 Purpose
The `artifacts/` folder is your go-to space for research outputs that drive decisions, strategy, and communication across teams.

## ✅ What Goes Here
- Insight cards
- Executive summaries
- Research briefs
- Synthesis readouts
- Presentation-ready findings

## 🔍 Why Keep It Separate from `research/`
| Benefit                  | Explanation                                                   |
|--------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Cross-study reuse        | Artifacts often synthesize themes across multiple studies     |
| Clearer stakeholder access | Decision-makers want quick access to polished outputs        |
| Reduced duplication      | Avoids scattering similar takeaways in multiple folders       |
| Repo hygiene             | Keeps raw research separate from strategic artifacts          |

## 💬 Use When…
- You’re creating insights for engineers, designers, PMs
- You’re preparing a portfolio, grant, or quarterly equity report
- You want others to find and cite your work easily

**Tip:** Think of `research/` as the “lab” and `artifacts/` as the “showroom.”
