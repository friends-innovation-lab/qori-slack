# 📁 README

---

## 🧭 Purpose

The `artifacts/` folder is where we store **research outputs** that have been synthesized, shared, or are ready to influence design and product decisions. Unlike raw research, these materials are polished and broadly consumable.

---

## 📦 What You’ll Find Here

- ✅ Insight Cards – Single-point learnings with evidence and recommendations
- ✅ Executive Summaries – 1-pagers for stakeholders and leaders
- ✅ Research Briefs – Setup docs outlining the purpose, goals, and scope of a study
- ✅ Final Readouts – Polished deliverables used to present findings
- ✅ Synthesis Outputs – Affinity maps, thematic frameworks, quotebooks

---

## 🤔 Why Use a Separate Folder?

| Benefit                  | Explanation                                                   |
|--------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| 🔁 Cross-study reuse      | Artifacts often summarize themes from multiple studies        |
| 🧠 Better decision access | Easier for PMs, engineers, and stakeholders to digest quickly |
| 🗃️ Repo hygiene           | Keeps research data separate from strategic outputs           |
| 🔗 Link-friendly          | Easier to reference directly in GitHub issues or presentations|

---

## 💡 Analogy

Think of `research/` as your **lab** where experiments happen,  
and `artifacts/` as your **showroom** where results are shared.

---

## 🧪 Use This Folder When…

- You’re creating insight cards or briefs that may support future work
- You’re preparing artifacts for stakeholder consumption or roadmap influence
- You’re reusing research in synthesis across projects
