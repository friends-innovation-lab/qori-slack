# 💡 Insight Card – Women Veterans & PTSD Access Study

## 🧭 Insight: Dense PTSD Pages Increase Cognitive Load

---

## 📝 Description  
Participants consistently described PTSD-related resource pages as too long and text-heavy, making it hard to process information, especially during emotionally difficult moments.

---

## 💬 Supporting Evidence  
> “If I’m anxious, I’m not reading three pages of links.” — P02  
> “Too many words, not enough help.” — P05

---

## 🎯 Why It Matters  
During crisis or stress, users have limited cognitive capacity. Dense content discourages action and undermines the sense of support and clarity.

---

## 🛠️ Recommendation  
Use bulleted summaries, visual anchors, and concise content blocks. Prioritize readability and structure content for skimming.
