# 📚 Quotebook – Women Veterans & PTSD Access Study

A curated set of quotes from participants that reflect recurring emotional tones, barriers, and opportunities observed in the study.

---

## 💬 Theme: Lack of Representation

> “It didn’t feel like it was talking to me. Like, it’s written for someone else.”  
— P02

> “I didn’t even know there was a section just for women.”  
— P01

**Why it matters:**  
Women Veterans felt the PTSD content lacked gender-specific relevance, leading to disengagement and mistrust.

---

## 💬 Theme: Information Overload

> “Too many words, not enough help.”  
— P05

> “If I’m anxious, I’m not reading three pages of links.”  
— P02

**Why it matters:**  
Participants with high emotional load struggled with dense, text-heavy content and needed more skimmable formats.

---

## 💬 Theme: Fear of Contacting Support

> “I don’t know who’s going to pick up the phone. That’s scary.”  
— P03

> “I’ve called before and got passed around.”  
— P01

**Why it matters:**  
When contact options feel vague or impersonal, trust breaks down and Veterans delay or abandon outreach.
