# 💡 Insight Card – Women Veterans & PTSD Access Study

## 🧭 Insight: Lack of Gender-Specific Pathways Creates Disconnect

---

## 📝 Description  
Participants did not encounter content that felt tailored to women Veterans or acknowledged their specific experiences with PTSD. This lack of visible representation led to confusion and a reduced sense of belonging or trust in the digital experience.

---

## 💬 Supporting Evidence  
> “It didn’t feel like it was talking to me. Like, it’s written for someone else.” — P02  
> “I didn’t even know there was a section just for women.” — P01

---

## 🎯 Why It Matters  
Without clear gender-specific signals, women Veterans may disengage before finding appropriate resources. This contradicts the VA’s equity goals and reduces uptake of critical mental health services.

---

## 🛠️ Recommendation  
Add persistent top-level and in-page links to the Women Veterans Health section. Include PTSD-specific subnavigation and more inclusive imagery and messaging.
