# 💡 Insight Card – Women Veterans & PTSD Access Study

## 🧭 Insight: Contact Information Lacks Clarity and Trust

---

## 📝 Description  
Participants expressed confusion or hesitation when prompted to call general support numbers. They did not know who would answer or what to expect, which reduced their likelihood of seeking help.

---

## 💬 Supporting Evidence  
> “I don’t know who’s going to pick up the phone. That’s scary.” — P03  
> “I’ve called before and got passed around.” — P01

---

## 🎯 Why It Matters  
Trust is especially critical in mental health access. Vague contact language or unknown pathways contribute to emotional friction and drop-off.

---

## 🛠️ Recommendation  
Label support numbers clearly. Indicate who’s answering, their hours, and what kind of support they provide. Use trauma-informed tone in all contact blocks.
