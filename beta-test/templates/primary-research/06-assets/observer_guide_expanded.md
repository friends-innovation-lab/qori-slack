# How to Be a Great Observer

👀 Thank you for joining a research session as an observer. Your role is critical to building empathy and improving the product — while keeping the session smooth and participant-centered.

---

## ✅ Before the Session

- Join the call 5 minutes early to test your audio and camera
- Read the [discussion guide](../03-fieldwork/discussion_guide.md) and participant context
- Clarify with the researcher what role you should play (silent observer or post-session debrief participant)

---

## 🚫 During the Session

- Stay on mute unless explicitly invited to speak
- Do not interrupt the participant or researcher
- Avoid using the chat feature unless instructed

---

## 🧠 After the Session

- Drop observations in the #research-synthesis Slack channel using clear, respectful language
- Avoid judgmental or leading language (e.g., say “Participant hesitated during login,” not “They were confused”)
- Focus on behavior, quotes, and emotional cues

---

## 💬 Feedback is Welcome

You’re not expected to be perfect — you're here to learn and support.
If you have questions about what you saw, bring them to the debrief or message the researcher directly.

---

## 🙏 Thank You

Thank you for helping build a more user-centered product.
You are strengthening our team’s insight muscle by showing up with curiosity and humility.
