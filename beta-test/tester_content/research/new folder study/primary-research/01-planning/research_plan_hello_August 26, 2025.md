# 🧭 Research Plan – hello

**📦 Product/Feature Area:** "hello - a user-friendly platform for Veterans to access their medical records and schedule appointments easily. This tool improves efficiency and accessibility within the VA healthcare system, ensuring Veterans receive timely and effective care."

**🏛️ Team/Office:** hello  
**👩🏽‍🔬 Lead Researcher:** hello  
**🗓️ Date:** August 26, 2025

---

## 🔎 Background

This research will inform the decision to enhance the user experience of the "hello" feature. This decision will simplify the process for Veterans to access important information quickly and efficiently, ultimately improving their overall experience with the VA. By streamlining this feature, we aim to increase user satisfaction and engagement with the platform.

---

## 🎯 OCTO Priorities

**Primary OCTO Priority:** Improve the Veteran login experience

**Explanation:** By focusing on improving the Veteran login experience, we can ensure that veterans have easy and secure access to the VA.gov platform.

**Additional Priorities (if applicable):**
- **Solidify the VA.gov platform:** Strengthening the foundation of VA.gov will provide a stable and reliable platform for veterans to access important resources.
- **Fast-track disability claims, starting with PACT Act claims:** Streamlining the disability claims process will help veterans receive the support they need in a timely manner.

---

## 🧭 User Journey

**Detected Audience:** Veterans

**User Journey Connection:** hello serves as a tool for Veterans in the "Putting down roots" life stage to connect with resources and services offered by the VA.

**Key Moments/Phases:**
- **Putting down roots:** Veterans use hello to explore housing options and community support services in their new location.
- **Accessing benefits:** Veterans utilize hello to navigate the process of applying for and managing their VA benefits.
- **Healthcare coordination:** Veterans use hello to schedule medical appointments and communicate with healthcare providers.

**Critical Pain Points (if applicable):** Ensuring that hello is user-friendly and accessible to Veterans who may not be tech-savvy or have limited digital literacy.

---

## 🎓 Research Goals

**Primary Goal:** Identify key pain points in the hello feature to improve user experience and increase user engagement.

**Supporting Goals:**
- Determine the most common user errors and obstacles encountered while using hello.
- Gather feedback on user preferences and expectations for hello through interviews.
- Identify opportunities for optimizing the hello feature to align with user needs and preferences.

---

## ✅ Success Criteria & Expected Deliverables

**Success Criteria:**
1. **Decision Support:** Research findings will directly inform the development of the new hello feature set within 2 weeks of completion. This will include clear recommendations for feature prioritization and design improvements based on user feedback.
  
2. **Quality Threshold:** Research findings will meet a minimum standard of 80% agreement among stakeholders on key insights and recommendations. Additionally, at least 70% of identified usability issues will be actionable and directly addressable by the product team.
  
3. **Timeline Success:** Research findings will be delivered to stakeholders within 1 week of completion, allowing for ample time for review and discussion before key decisions need to be made. Stakeholders will have a maximum of 2 weeks to provide feedback and align on next steps.
  
4. **Impact Measurement:** The success of the research will be measured by a 20% increase in user satisfaction scores for the hello feature set within 3 months of implementation. This will be tracked through user feedback surveys and analytics data.

**Key Outputs:**
**Primary Deliverables:**
- Usability report with actionable insights - delivered within 3 days of research completion
- Interview transcripts and key findings summary - delivered within 2 days of research completion
- Recommendations presentation highlighting key opportunities for improvement - delivered within 4 days of research completion

**Supporting Materials:**
- User personas and journey maps - delivered within 5 days of research completion
- Video highlights reel showcasing user interactions - delivered within 3 days of research completion

---

## 🛠️ Methodology

**Methodology Rationale:** Usability testing and interviews are chosen for their direct feedback on user interactions and insights into user needs, aligning with the research goal of improving product usability.

**Implementation Approach:**
- Conducting 1-hour usability testing sessions with 5 participants, followed by in-depth interviews for deeper understanding.
- Utilizing task-based scenarios and open-ended questions to gather specific feedback on hello features and functionality.
- Ensuring VA Compliance through timely collaboration cycles, trauma-informed approaches, and accessibility accommodations for all participants.

## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

**Recruitment challenges: Difficulty finding qualified participants for usability testing and interviews**
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Utilize multiple recruitment channels such as social media, online communities, and professional networks to reach a diverse pool of potential participants. Offer incentives to increase participation rates.

**Execution issues: Problems with the implementation of usability testing and interviews**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Conduct pilot tests of the usability testing and interview protocols to identify and address any potential issues before full implementation. Provide training to team members involved in conducting the research to ensure consistency and quality.

**Timeline risks: Delays that could impact the extended timeline of the project**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Develop a detailed project plan with clear milestones and deadlines. Regularly monitor progress and adjust resources or priorities as needed to stay on track. Communicate any potential delays to stakeholders early and proactively.

**Quality concerns: Data validity and stakeholder satisfaction**
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Mitigation:** Implement rigorous quality control measures such as double-checking data, conducting member checks with participants, and regularly reviewing findings with stakeholders to ensure alignment. Prioritize clear and actionable insights to maximize stakeholder satisfaction.
