# Executive Summary

Source: VA Office of Information Technology, Digital Experience Analytics
Report Period: January 2024 - December 2024
Sample Size: 2.3M unique veteran users

Analysis of veteran digital behavior across VA web and mobile platforms reveals significant mobile adoption growth and identifies key usability barriers affecting service delivery.

# Mobile Usage Statistics:

- 78% of VA interactions now occur on mobile devices (up from 52% in 2022)
- Average session duration: 4.2 minutes (mobile) vs 8.7 minutes (desktop)
- Task abandonment rate: 42% mobile vs 28% desktop
- Most common mobile tasks: Prescription refills (89%), appointment viewing (76%), payment checking (61%)

# Demographic Insights:

- Veterans 65+: Fastest growing mobile user segment (156% increase)
- Rural veterans: 23% more likely to use mobile-only access
- Veterans with service-connected disabilities: 67% rely primarily on mobile access

# Pain Points Identified:

- Navigation Confusion: 34% of support calls related to "can't find feature"
- Form Completion Issues: 28% abandonment rate on multi-step processes
- Search Functionality: Only 12% success rate for finding specific information
