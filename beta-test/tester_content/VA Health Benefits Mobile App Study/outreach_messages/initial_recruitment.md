Study Details
Recruitment Details: We're conducting research to improve the VA Health and Benefits mobile app navigation and make it easier for veterans to find and use important features. The study involves a 60-minute virtual session where you'll use the mobile app to complete common tasks like checking appointments, managing prescriptions, and accessing benefits information. Your feedback will directly help us identify navigation barriers and design better mobile experiences for veterans accessing VA services on their phones and tablets.
Recruitment Amount: $75

Sign Up Instructions: To participate in this research study, please reply to this email with your availability for a 60-minute virtual session over the next two weeks.
