## Participant Criteria

**Must-haves:**
- Currently have an active VA disability claim or have filed a claim within the past 12 months
- Have used VA.gov or eBenefits to check claim status at least once

**Nice-to-haves:**
- Mix of claim types (initial claims, appeals, supplemental claims)
- Variety of claim statuses (in progress, pending decision, completed)
- Range of digital comfort levels

**Exclude:**
- VA employees or contractors
- Participated in VA research studies within the past 6 months
- Unable to access a computer or mobile device for the session

## Success Criteria

• We will have succeeded if participants can locate their claims status information within 2-3 clicks from the main landing page without assistance

• We will have succeeded if at least 80% of users can successfully navigate to and understand their claims status without requiring help or clarification

• We will have succeeded if we identify and document the top 3-5 navigation pain points that prevent users from efficiently accessing their claims information

• We will have succeeded if participants can complete the claims status lookup task in under 2 minutes on average

---

## Expected Outcomes

Research will inform:
- **Information Architecture** — Redesign of claims status page hierarchy and navigation pathways to reduce user confusion and improve task completion rates
- **User Interface Design** — Development of clearer status indicators, progress bars, and wayfinding elements that help users understand their current location and next steps in the claims process
- **Content Strategy** — Creation of more intuitive labeling, messaging, and help content that addresses common navigation pain points and reduces support ticket volume
