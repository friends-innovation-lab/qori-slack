## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans are experiencing confusion or frustration in the process of using the Slack > Slash Command > Github flow.
- Which steps in the user flow are causing the most delays or errors for veterans.
- Why veterans may be hesitant to adopt this process and what barriers are preventing its successful implementation.
- How veterans are currently navigating the Slack > Slash Command > Github flow and where improvements can be made for a smoother experience.
