## 🎯 Business Context

The study aims to investigate the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and veterans in the context of testing procedures. By exploring the specific obstacles and needs of this population, the research seeks to improve testing protocols and services tailored to VA and veteran communities. Understanding the nuances of testing experiences for VA and veterans is crucial in enhancing the overall effectiveness and accessibility of healthcare services for this demographic.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Limited accessibility to testing facilities is causing delays in receiving necessary medical evaluations for Veterans
- Inadequate testing procedures are leading to inaccurate diagnoses and treatment plans for Veterans
- Outdated testing technologies are hindering the efficiency and accuracy of test results for Veterans

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans encounter obstacles or frustrations during the testing process.
- Which aspects of the user flow are most critical for successful testing.
- Why certain barriers exist in the testing process for veterans.
- How veterans currently navigate the testing process and what challenges they face.

## 🎯 Target Barriers

- Users struggle to find the testing feature within the interface due to poor visibility and lack of clear labeling.
- Users are unsure of the steps required to conduct a test, leading to confusion and frustration during the process.
- Users encounter technical issues when trying to run tests, such as slow loading times or error messages that disrupt the testing workflow.
- Users are not provided with sufficient guidance or instructions on how to interpret test results, hindering their ability to make informed decisions based on the data.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. "How do veterans currently navigate the process of scheduling and attending medical tests within the VA healthcare system?"
2. "What barriers prevent veterans from completing necessary medical tests within the recommended timeframe?"
3. "Why do veterans experience delays or obstacles in accessing and receiving test results from healthcare providers within the VA system?"
4. "What causes confusion or frustration for veterans when it comes to understanding the importance of follow-up testing and procedures recommended by healthcare professionals?"
