## 🎯 Business Context

In order to address the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and veterans, this study aims to conduct comprehensive testing. By examining specific issues within this population, we seek to improve understanding and develop targeted solutions to enhance the overall support and services provided to VA and veterans. This research endeavor is crucial in ensuring that the needs of this demographic are met effectively and efficiently.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Limited access to testing facilities is hindering Veterans from participating in the study, potentially skewing research results
- Inadequate communication about the study is leading to low participation rates among Veterans, impacting the validity of the research findings
- Technical glitches in the testing platform are causing frustration among Veterans, affecting their overall experience and willingness to engage with the study

## 🎯 Research Objectives

What we want to learn:
- Where veterans encounter the most significant barriers when navigating the system.
- Which barriers are the most critical for veterans to overcome.
- Why veterans are experiencing these barriers in the first place.
- How veterans currently interact with the system and where improvements can be made.

## 🎯 Target Barriers

- Users struggle to find the search bar on the homepage, leading to difficulty in locating specific products.
- The checkout process lacks a progress indicator, causing confusion about the number of steps remaining to complete a purchase.
- Users encounter error messages without clear instructions on how to resolve issues, resulting in frustration and abandonment of tasks.
- The mobile version of the website has overlapping elements, making it challenging to navigate and interact with content effectively.
- Users face slow loading times when accessing product images, impacting the overall browsing experience and leading to decreased engagement.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. "What barriers prevent veterans from accessing necessary resources and support services?"
2. "Why do veterans experience challenges navigating the system of available resources and support services?"
3. "What causes veterans to feel overwhelmed or discouraged when seeking assistance for their needs?"
4. "How do veterans currently navigate the process of seeking help for mental health issues, and what obstacles do they encounter along the way?"

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
1. The lack of user-friendly navigation on the website increases the bounce rate among potential customers.
2. Veterans with PTSD face more difficulties accessing and utilizing the online support resources provided by the service.
3. The current design of the mobile app leads to decreased user engagement and retention rates.
