# Research Project Details

## Basic Information

- **Name:** VA Telehealth Onboarding and Appointment Experience Study

**Description:**  
User research project focused on the full telehealth journey, from scheduling a video visit to successfully joining and completing the appointment. The work centers on how Veterans receive instructions, prepare their devices, handle access links, and navigate last-minute technical issues across VA.gov, My HealtheVet, VA Video Connect, and related emails or text messages.

The goal is to reduce appointment no-shows, lower technical failure rates, and increase Veteran confidence when using telehealth as an option for care.
