## Study Details
**Recruitment Details:**  
We're conducting a study to better understand how Veterans prepare for their VA telehealth appointments. Many Veterans experience challenges during setup such as running device tests, understanding instructions, or joining the visit at the right time. Your feedback will help us simplify the process, improve accessibility, and make it easier for Veterans to feel confident before and during their telehealth appointments. The study includes a 60-minute virtual session where we'll walk through a simulated telehealth visit together.
