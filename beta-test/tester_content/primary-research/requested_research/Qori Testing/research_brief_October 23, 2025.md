## 🎯 Business Context

Study Context: "Qori Testing"

In response to the unique challenges faced by Veterans Affairs (VA) and Veterans, this study aims to conduct Qori testing to assess the effectiveness of current interventions and identify areas for improvement in providing care and support to this population. By examining the outcomes of Qori testing within the VA system, we seek to enhance the quality of services offered to Veterans and address any gaps or barriers that may exist.

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical Issues Driving This Research**
> 
> - Difficulty in navigating the Qori Testing system is causing frustration for Veterans trying to access important health information
- Inefficient Qori Testing processes are leading to delays in delivering test results to Veterans, impacting timely healthcare decisions
- Lack of user-friendly design in the Qori Testing interface is preventing Veterans from easily inputting and retrieving their health data

## 🎯 Target Barriers

- Users struggle to navigate the Qori Testing interface due to unclear labeling and organization of features.
- The lack of visual hierarchy in the interface makes it difficult for users to prioritize tasks and find important information.
- Users experience delays and errors when submitting test results due to a slow and unreliable server connection.
- Inconsistent design elements across different sections of Qori Testing cause confusion and hinder user efficiency.
- Users are unable to customize their testing preferences and settings, leading to frustration and inefficiency in their testing process.

## 🤔 Key Questions & Hypotheses

### 🔍 Primary Research Questions
1. How do veterans currently navigate the user flow of Qori Testing from initial sign-up to receiving their test results?
2. What barriers prevent veterans from completing the Qori Testing process in a timely and efficient manner?
3. Why do veterans experience difficulties in understanding the instructions and requirements of Qori Testing?
4. What causes veterans to feel frustrated or overwhelmed when using the Qori Testing platform?

### 💭 Team Hypotheses
1. The lack of user-friendly interface in Qori Testing increases the likelihood of errors in test results.
2. Veterans with PTSD face more difficulty in navigating the Qori Testing platform due to its complex layout.
3. The current design of Qori Testing leads to decreased user engagement and lower completion rates of assessments.
