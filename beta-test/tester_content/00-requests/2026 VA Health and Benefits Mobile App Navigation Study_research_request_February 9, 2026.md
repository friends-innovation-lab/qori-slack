# Research Request: 2026 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App Navigation Study

**Submitted by:**  | **Date:** February 9, 2026 | **Urgency:** high

---

## The Problem

The VA mobile app has a 4.2/10 user satisfaction rating and a 45% task abandonment rate. Veterans frequently rely on search to locate basic features like prescription refills and appointment scheduling, which indicates poor information architecture. Analytics reveal that 60% of users who abandon tasks resort to calling the VA help line instead.

---

## Affected Users

Veterans ages 25-75+ who use the VA mobile app on iOS and Android devices for health and benefits management, including users with accessibility needs who rely on VoiceOver, TalkBack, and large text features.

---

## Decisions This Will Inform

• **Navigation architecture**: Choose between bottom tab navigation vs. hamburger menu structure, and finalize information hierarchy to optimize user flow and content discoverability

• **Interface optimization**: Determine optimal button placement, sizing, and positioning to improve task completion rates and reduce user friction

• **Accessibility compliance**: Implement specific screen reader enhancements, alt-text standards, and WCAG 2.1 AA requirements to ensure inclusive user experience
