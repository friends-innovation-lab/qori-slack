# Research Request: 2027 VA Health and Benefits Mobile App Navigation Study

**Submitted by:**  | **Date:** February 10, 2026 | **Urgency:** high

---

## The Problem

The VA mobile app suffers from poor usability, with a 4.2/10 user satisfaction rating and a 45% task abandonment rate. Veterans struggle to locate basic features like prescription refills and appointment scheduling through the app's navigation, forcing them to rely on search functionality instead. This poor information architecture drives 60% of users who abandon tasks to call the VA help line as an alternative.

---

## Affected Users

Veterans aged 25-75+ who use the VA mobile app on iOS and Android devices for health and benefits management, including users with accessibility needs who rely on VoiceOver, TalkBack, and large text features.

---

## Decisions This Will Inform

• **Navigation architecture**: Choose between bottom tab navigation or hamburger menu structure, and finalize information hierarchy for primary user flows

• **Interface optimization**: Determine optimal placement for primary action buttons (CTA positioning, size, and visual prominence)

• **Accessibility compliance**: Implement specific screen reader improvements including alt text standards, focus order, and ARIA label requirements
