## 🔍 Research Overview
This study aimed to understand how veterans navigate the online benefits application system and identify common points of confusion.

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

### 1. Eligibility Uncertainty
> “I wasn't sure if I even qualified.”

- Users often couldn't determine their eligibility based on the current site copy.
- **Recommendation:** Add a clear yes/no eligibility tool.

### 2. Overwhelming Layout
> “It felt like a wall of text.”

- The benefits form lacks scannable structure.
- **Recommendation:** Use accordions or step-by-step design.

### 3. Redundant Document Uploads
> “I uploaded the same thing three times.”

- The upload interface caused frustration with repeated asks.
- **Recommendation:** Store and pre-fill documents when possible.
